TITEL:
Henry Cotton — The Doctor Who Cut Out Insanity One Organ at a Time

BESCHREIBUNG:
In the wards of Trenton State Hospital, a physician convinced that madness lived in the body's hidden corners began pulling it out, tooth by tooth, organ by organ. Henry Cotton believed insanity was a disease of infection, not the mind, and for two decades he chased that theory into the surgical chair, extracting teeth, tonsils, colons, and reproductive organs from thousands of patients who never consented to become proof of his conviction. This episode of Anatomy of the Damned: Physicians Who Butchered to Heal traces Cotton's rise from respected reformer to the architect of an assembly line of mutilation, sanctioned by science, praised by colleagues, and hidden behind hospital walls. Listeners will move through the wards themselves, hearing how a man determined to cure suffering became indistinguishable from the horror he claimed to fight, and how the numbers behind his "cures" were quietly buried for decades. This is a story about certainty without limits, and the terrible cost of a doctor who never doubted himself.
