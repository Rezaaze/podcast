TITEL:
Empress Dowager Cixi — The Voice Behind the Silk Screen

BESCHREIBUNG:
She rules an empire of four hundred million souls without ever sitting in open court — her voice filtered through silk, her face hidden from the men who serve her. This episode of Phantom Thrones: Sovereigns of the Silent Halls traces the ascent of Empress Dowager Cixi, a concubine who claws her way through the perfumed corridors of the Forbidden City to become the true power behind China's crumbling throne. Listen as palace whispers curdle into conspiracy, as loyalty becomes a currency spent in shadows, and as one woman learns to rule a nation she was never permitted to touch directly. Foreign armies mass at the gates. Rebellion stirs in the countryside. And behind every lacquered screen, decisions are made that will echo through the final decades of imperial China. This is a portrait of absolute power wielded in absolute isolation — magnificent, suffocating, and utterly alone.
