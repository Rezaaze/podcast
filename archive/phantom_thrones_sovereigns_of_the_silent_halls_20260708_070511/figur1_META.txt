TITEL:
Tiberius — The Emperor Who Ruled an Empire from the Shadows of Capri

BESCHREIBUNG:
An emperor who conquered the known world could not conquer his own mind—so he fled it, retreating to a cliff-top fortress and ruling Rome by letter alone. This episode of Phantom Thrones: Sovereigns of the Silent Halls follows Tiberius as he abandons the Senate, the crowds, and the marble halls of power for the sheer isolation of Capri, governing an empire he refuses to see. You will walk the villa's stone corridors where whispers replace commands, where spies carry the only proof that the emperor still breathes, and where absence itself becomes a weapon of control. As years pass without his face appearing in Rome, the court transforms into a haunted bureaucracy awaiting a master who may never return. This is a portrait of power stripped of presence—a study in paranoia, distance, and the terrifying silence of a throne ruled by rumor. Step into the shadows of history's first phantom emperor.
