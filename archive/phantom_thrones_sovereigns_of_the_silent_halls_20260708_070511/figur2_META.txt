TITEL:
Charles VI — The King Who Believed He Was Made of Glass

BESCHREIBUNG:
In a shadowed forest outside Le Mans, a king turns on his own men without warning, and the France he ruled will never recover. This episode of Phantom Thrones: Sovereigns of the Silent Halls follows Charles VI of France as an incomprehensible fracture in his mind convinces him his body is fashioned from glass, brittle enough to shatter at the slightest touch. For three decades he reigns as a hollow ornament, propped upright by a wife who watches from the shadows, a brother hungry for power, and uncles circling like carrion birds. Behind sealed letters and courtly smiles, the palace becomes a labyrinth of whispered plots, forged loyalties, and a throne quietly stolen in plain sight. As factions harden into deadly rivalry, the kingdom itself begins to splinter along the same fault lines as the king's fragile mind. This is a portrait of power hollowed out from within, of madness weaponized, and of a crown that shattered a nation.
