TITEL:
Ibrahim I — The Sultan Raised in the Shadow of Death

BESCHREIBUNG:
He spent his childhood waiting for the executioner's cord, and it never left him. Ibrahim I survived what none of his brothers did: years locked inside the Ottoman cage, listening for footsteps that meant death, until the empire itself had no one left to crown but him. In this episode of Phantom Thrones: Sovereigns of the Silent Halls, we descend into the mind of a sultan who inherited an empire he was never permitted to prepare for, and who answered that terror with an obsession for sable furs, a harem swollen past reason, and a devotion to his mother that blurred the line between son and puppet. Palace informants, half-lit corridors, and the hush of Topkapi at night guide this portrait of a throne built on fear and paranoia, and the silent machinery of the Sultanate of Women that watched, waited, and ultimately decided his fate.
