TITEL:
Phantom Thrones: Five Rulers, Five Silences, One Long Descent Into Isolation

BESCHREIBUNG:
Power, at its most absolute, often ends in a locked room. This anthology gathers five monarchs separated by centuries and continents, united only by the strange gravity of solitude that overtook their reigns. An emperor withdraws to a cliffside island and rules an empire through letters no one can question. A king wakes convinced his body is glass and spends thirty years as a hollow crown passed between grasping hands. A boy raised in the shadow of execution ascends a throne he was never meant to survive, and unravels inside it. A young monarch abandons a modernizing nation for castles built by candlelight and music only he can hear. And behind a silk screen, a dowager empress commands the largest empire on Earth through whispers alone, unseen by the very officials who obey her.

Phantom Thrones: Sovereigns of the Silent Halls traces how proximity to absolute power can hollow out the mind that holds it, and how courts, dynasties, and empires learn to function around a ruler who has quietly disappeared into themselves. Told as one continuous journey across five reigns and five very different kinds of vanishing, this is a study in paranoia, ceremony, and the eerie machinery that keeps moving long after the person at its center has gone silent.
