TITEL:
Ludwig II — The King Who Vanished Into His Own Fairy Tale

BESCHREIBUNG:
He inherited a throne he never asked for, and answered it by disappearing into a world only he could see. Ludwig II of Bavaria turned his back on the iron discipline of a unifying Germany, retreating instead into the swelling myths of Wagner's operas and the moonlit turrets of castles built for an audience of one. In this episode of Phantom Thrones: Sovereigns of the Silent Halls, we trace the slow unraveling of a king who chose fantasy over governance, watching as ministers who never once examined him quietly began writing the paperwork of his madness. We walk the candlelit corridors of Neuschwanstein, a monument to escape disguised as architecture, and follow Ludwig to the edge of a dark Bavarian lake where the boundary between monarch and myth finally dissolves. This is a story of obsession, isolation, and a crown too heavy for a man who preferred the company of ghosts and swans to the burdens of a modern nation.
