TITEL:
Armand Hammer — The Doctor Who Sold His Soul for Oil and Legacy

BESCHREIBUNG:
He walked into Kremlin offices with a doctor's black bag and left with deals worth empires. Armand Hammer spent a lifetime blurring the line between physician, financier, and statesman, charming Lenin's Soviet Union decades before he ever touched a barrel of crude. But it was in the scorched deserts of Gaddafi's Libya that Hammer transformed a failing shell company into Occidental Petroleum, striking concessions so audacious they cracked the foundations of the international oil cartel. Behind the boardroom triumphs lurked whispered bribes, political favors, and a paper trail that investigators could never quite close. As his fortune grew, so did his hunger for something money alone couldn't buy: immortality, pursued through galleries, museums, and a collection built to outlive the empire that funded it. This episode of Black Gold Empires: The Men Who Bled the Earth Dry traces how ambition, access, and obsession fused into one man's relentless pursuit of legend — and what it ultimately cost him.
