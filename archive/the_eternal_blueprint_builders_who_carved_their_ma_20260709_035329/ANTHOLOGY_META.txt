TITEL:
The Eternal Blueprint: Builders Who Carved Their Madness in Stone

BESCHREIBUNG:
Some men do not build monuments. They become them, sacrificing armies of laborers, entire treasuries, and their own sanity to raise something that will outlast the flesh that dreamed it. The Eternal Blueprint is a complete anthology, three sovereigns, three centuries, three continents, bound together in one continuous descent into the psychology of absolute power expressed in brick, marble, and stone.

A god-king on the Nile conscripts a nation to defy death itself with impossible geometry. A grieving emperor empties a Mughal treasury to entomb love in white marble, blind to the cost closing in around him. A twentieth-century dictator flattens a living city to erect a monument to his own image, deaf to the empire crumbling beneath his feet. Each ruler believed stone could silence mortality, family, and the judgment of history. Each discovered that the very obsession which raised their masterpiece was already carving the architecture of their own undoing.

This is not a history of buildings. It is a forensic study of ambition metastasizing into delusion, told across one uninterrupted journey through scaffolding, quarries, and palace corridors where power slowly forgets it was ever human. The blueprints survive. The builders did not escape so easily.
