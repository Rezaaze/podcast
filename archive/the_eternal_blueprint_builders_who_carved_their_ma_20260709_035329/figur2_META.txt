TITEL:
Shah Jahan — The Emperor Who Built a Tomb and Died Watching It

BESCHREIBUNG:
An emperor drains a kingdom's treasury to bury his grief in marble, and the stone begins to bury him back. This episode of The Eternal Blueprint: Builders Who Carved Their Madness in Stone traces Shah Jahan's descent from grieving husband to obsessive architect, commanding twenty thousand hands to raise a mausoleum so perfect it consumes the empire meant to outlive it. We walk the scaffolding of ambition alongside the ledgers of ruin, watching symmetry become tyranny and love calcify into monument. As the treasury empties and his sons circle the throne, the emperor's masterpiece becomes the very thing that isolates him from the world he built it to impress. This is a story of devotion twisted into obsession, of a father who could design eternity in stone but could not read the ambition rising in his own blood. What remains is a question the marble still asks: what does it cost to build something that will never die.
