TITEL:
Nicolae Ceaușescu — The Palace Built on a Buried City

BESCHREIBUNG:
A tyrant looked at the heart of his own capital and saw only rubble waiting to happen — so he set out to build one god a house, and buried a city to do it. In this episode of The Eternal Blueprint: Builders Who Carved Their Madness in Stone, we walk the ruins of Nicolae Ceaușescu's obsession: the neighborhoods erased overnight, the churches dragged away on rails, the exhausted soldiers and starving citizens conscripted to raise walls of marble for a leader who believed concrete could outlast conscience. You will stand on the balcony where his power first began to crack, trace the endless corridors of a palace built for a personality cult with no audience left to applaud it, and watch the widening gap between one man's vision and the nation forced to carry it on its back. This is a story of architecture as delusion, of a capital sacrificed for a monument, and of ambition measured in square meters rather than lives. The palace still stands. The cost of standing it there is another matter entirely.
