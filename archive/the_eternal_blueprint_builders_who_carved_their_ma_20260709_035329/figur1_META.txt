TITEL:
Khufu — The God-King Who Built a Mountain to Cheat Death

BESCHREIBUNG:
A king stares into the void of his own mortality and answers with two million tons of stone. This is the story of Khufu, the Fourth Dynasty pharaoh who turned an entire kingdom into a single machine built for one purpose: eternity. Follow the quarrymen dragging limestone across a bled and exhausted empire, the architects who wielded geometry like a weapon against oblivion, and the obsessive precision that pushed Egypt to the edge of collapse. As the Great Pyramid rises on the Giza plateau, something else begins to hollow out beneath its perfect angles, a silence that no inscription can explain and no treasury can afford. This episode of The Eternal Blueprint traces how absolute power, aimed at defying death itself, curdles into something far stranger inside the walls it raised. What remains is a name worn smooth by sand, and a monument that still refuses to answer the questions it was built to silence.
