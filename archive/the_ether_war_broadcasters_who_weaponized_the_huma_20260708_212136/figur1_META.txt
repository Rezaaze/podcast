TITEL:
William Joyce — The Voice That Hunted Britain in the Dark

BESCHREIBUNG:
Every night during the Blitz, a voice slipped through the static into British homes — clipped, aristocratic, and impossible to ignore. It called itself Germany Calling. The British called it something crueler: Lord Haw-Haw. Behind the microphone stood William Joyce, a fascist true believer who turned shortwave radio into a weapon of dread, needling a nation's fears with rumor, half-truth, and a drawl borrowed from a class he never truly belonged to. Millions tuned in nightly, torn between mockery and unease, unable to look away from a voice that seemed to know too much. This episode of The Ether War traces Joyce's rise from obscure agitator to the most infamous broadcaster of the war, and the slow unraveling of his certainty as the empire he served began to crumble, static creeping into his voice until history itself came calling.
