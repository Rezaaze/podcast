TITEL:
Iva Toguri — The Woman Who Became a Ghost Named Tokyo Rose

BESCHREIBUNG:
Tokyo Rose never existed — and yet an entire nation swore they heard her voice, seducing dying soldiers with honeyed treachery over the airwaves. In The Ether War: Broadcasters Who Weaponized the Human Mind, this episode unravels how a stranded Japanese-American woman named Iva Toguri became the vessel for a myth she never asked to embody. Trapped behind enemy lines after Pearl Harbor, coerced into broadcasting under threat, she found herself hunted not for what she said, but for what a frightened America desperately needed to believe. Follow the manufacture of a villain from wartime rumor to courtroom spectacle, where evidence bent beneath the weight of collective panic and a nation's hunger for someone to blame. This is a story about propaganda that worked in both directions, enemy and ally alike, and about how fear can conjure monsters more convincing than any truth. What happens when the myth outlives the woman who was forced to wear its mask?
