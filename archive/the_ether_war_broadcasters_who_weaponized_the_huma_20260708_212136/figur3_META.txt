TITEL:
Georges Ruggiu — The Foreigner Who Turned a Microphone Into a Machete

BESCHREIBUNG:
A Belgian nobody walks into a Kigali radio booth and discovers the one thing he was always good at: making people believe. This episode of The Ether War traces how Georges Ruggiu, a failed DJ from Verviers, remade himself as the fanatical foreign voice of Radio Télévision Libre des Mille Collines, blending pop songs, call-in banter, and casual cruelty into a broadcast that guided killers street by street. You will hear how a nation's airwaves became a weapon, how names read aloud became death sentences, and how one hundred days of static and slaughter turned a struggling announcer into an instrument of annihilation. We follow the seduction of belonging, the intoxication of power over a captive audience, and the slow unraveling of a mind that could not outrun what it had unleashed. This is the story of how hate found its perfect frequency, and the reckoning that followed for the man who gave it a voice.
