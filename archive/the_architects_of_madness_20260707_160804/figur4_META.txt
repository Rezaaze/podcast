TITEL:
Rudolf II — The Hermit King Who Burned His Empire for the Philosopher's Stone

BESCHREIBUNG:
Behind the walls of Prague Castle, an emperor turned his back on the world to chase the secrets of eternity. Rudolf II inherited one of the mightiest crowns in Europe, and instead of ruling it, he retreated into shadow, surrounding himself with astrologers, alchemists, and the strange machinery of obsession. As his ministers begged for decisions and his enemies gathered at the borders, he sealed himself inside chambers filled with furnaces, star charts, and half-finished experiments to transmute lead into gold. This episode of The Architects of Madness follows his descent from sovereign to recluse, tracing how ambition curdled into paranoia, how brilliance blurred into delusion, and how a man who wanted to master the universe slowly lost his grip on his own kingdom. You will walk through candlelit laboratories, court intrigue, and the mounting fury of a bloodline that could no longer wait. What remains is a portrait of power dissolving into obsession, and the haunting question of what a king becomes when the crown stops mattering.
