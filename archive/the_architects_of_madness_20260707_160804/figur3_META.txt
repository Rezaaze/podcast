TITEL:
John Nash — Decoding a World Only He Could See

BESCHREIBUNG:
A Nobel laureate stares at a newspaper and sees the architecture of a global conspiracy hidden inside stock prices and headlines. This episode of The Architects of Madness follows John Nash from the sterile brilliance of Princeton's blackboards into a private universe where numbers whisper secrets meant only for him. You will walk beside a mind capable of reshaping game theory and economics, then watch that same mind turn its genius toward decoding messages no one else can read. Colleagues grow uneasy. Corridors become surveillance routes. The line between insight and delusion dissolves until locked wards, cold treatments, and silence become the only response anyone has left. This is a story about the terrifying proximity of genius and madness, about a man who mapped equilibrium into the chaos of human behavior while losing his own. It asks what happens when the sharpest mind alive becomes convinced the world is speaking directly to him, and whether escaping that certainty is possible at all.
