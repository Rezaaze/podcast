TITEL:
Palaces of the Mind: The Architects of Madness

BESCHREIBUNG:
Power does not corrupt loudly. It corrupts in silence, in empty rooms, in minds too vast for the world that built them. This is the complete story of four men who reached the outer edge of human achievement, and kept walking past it. A visionary who tamed electricity itself until his own rituals consumed him. An aviator and industrialist who conquered the sky only to vanish into a fortress built from fear. A mathematician who cracked the equations governing nations while a private war raged inside his own skull. And an emperor who traded his throne for alchemy, sealing himself inside a castle in search of a secret the universe never intended to surrender. The Architects of Madness follows these four descents as one unbroken journey, a single continuous night moving from laboratory to boardroom, to war room, to castle tower, tracing how brilliance and isolation feed on each other until nothing separates the genius from the ghost he becomes. Each man believed his mind could master the world. Each discovered, in his own way, what that mastery costs. Watch closely. Somewhere between ambition and ruin, the same pattern keeps repeating.
