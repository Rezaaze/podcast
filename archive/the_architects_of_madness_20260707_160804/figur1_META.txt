TITEL:
Nikola Tesla — Lord of Lightning, Prisoner of Silence

BESCHREIBUNG:
He tamed the lightning and believed he could illuminate the world without wires — but the same mind that split the sky in two would eventually fracture beyond repair. This episode of The Architects of Madness follows Nikola Tesla from his triumphant duel against Edison's currents to the cathedral-quiet laboratories where he chased cosmic frequencies no one else could hear. You will walk through crackling coils and shattered partnerships, through Wardenclyffe's rusting ambition and the fractured trusts that stripped him of empire and fortune alike. But deeper still, you will enter the locked hotel rooms where genius curdled into compulsion, where counting rituals, sterile silence, and an unbearable fear of germs consumed the man who once commanded electricity itself. This is not simply a story of invention. It is a descent into isolation, where visionary brilliance and crippling obsession share the same restless mind, and where the cost of seeing further than anyone else becomes impossible to ignore.
