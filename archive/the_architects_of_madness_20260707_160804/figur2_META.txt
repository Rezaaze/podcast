TITEL:
Howard Hughes — The Billionaire Who Barricaded Himself From the World

BESCHREIBUNG:
He was once the fastest man alive, a billionaire who built airplanes and broke his own bones proving it. Then something in Howard Hughes began to unravel — the sound of his own engines still ringing in his skull long after he stopped flying. This episode of The Architects of Madness traces the terrifying arc from the golden aviator adored by the world to the trembling recluse who could not bear the touch of another human hand. You will follow him from the wreckage of shattered flights into the sealed rooms of the Desert Inn, where germs became monsters and silence became a kind of armor. Every precaution he took to protect himself only tightened the walls around him, until the man who once owned the sky could no longer stand to be touched by air itself. What remains is a portrait of obsession consuming a legend from the inside out, one blackout curtain at a time.
