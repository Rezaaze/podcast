TITEL:
Ada — The Locket's Accusation

BESCHREIBUNG:
Ada's fingers force open a tin marked 'Discharged Effects' and find a silver locket—the very one she watched vanish from the ward. But the ledger says its owner was discharged alive and thriving elsewhere. In this episode of First, Do Harm, Ada traces the impossible gap between the official record and what her hands now hold, discovering that the ward's integrity rests on a lie that reaches from the apothecary's locked cabinet to the consultation room itself. Each person she confronts—from Wycliffe's ashen silence to figures waiting in shadowed doorways—reveals they've been carrying this knowledge in isolation. Listen as Ada discovers what the locket has been trying to tell her, and what happens when institutional secrets collide with personal truth.

FRAGE:
If you found evidence that something you trusted had hidden a darker truth, who would you tell first—and why?
