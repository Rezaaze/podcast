TITEL:
Moorhouse — when the ledger finally speaks

BESCHREIBUNG:
Moorhouse doesn't deny it. When Ada finally breaks through the silence, his confession is steady, unhesitant, and utterly unrepentant—just the beginning of the horror to come. In First, Do Harm's season finale, the man at the centre of the ward's darkest secret sits down to explain how medicine has always demanded sacrifices that no one wants named. Standing among the instruments that bear witness to everything, he reframes himself as merely part of a history longer than any individual conscience. But confession isn't reckoning. As the inspector's letter arrives with its careful language of 'irregularities' and the board convenes for its toothless inquiry, the episode's real terror emerges: power doesn't break under the weight of truth—it simply absorbs it, rewrites it, and carries on. This is a story about what happens when the cost of honesty is nothing at all.
