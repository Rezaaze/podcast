TITEL:
Ada — When She Understood the Debt

BESCHREIBUNG:
In this episode of First, Do Harm, Ada faces the moment when her last hope becomes her greatest threat. Moorhouse doesn't raise his voice when he mentions the gambling debts that vanished, the discharge papers that started appearing in the ledger the same month his colleague's obligations disappeared. He doesn't need to raise his voice. The story writes itself in numbers.

Ada watches the only person she might have trusted collapse into something far more dangerous — a man so indebted that he can't refuse what's being asked of him. When Moorhouse leans close with an offer that sounds like escape, everything Ada thought she understood about survival becomes a trap. The reckoning she didn't see coming arrives before she can breathe.

FRAGE:
How do you know who to trust when the person offering help might need something from you in return?
