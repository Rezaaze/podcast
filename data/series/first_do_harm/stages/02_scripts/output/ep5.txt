--- PART 1 ---

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: flat, unflinching]
How many, Dr. Moorhouse?

[NARRATOR]
The ward office. Wycliffe's confession still hangs in the air.

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: pressing, procedural]
How many women are behind that wall?

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: calm, unbothered]
Enough to matter.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: dry, almost bored]
And not enough to stop.

[SFX: pen set down on wood desk]

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: quiet, stunned]
Say that again.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: mild, dismissive]
You heard me the first time, Nurse Kirwin.

[DR_JONAS_WYCLIFFE | style: barely audible, shaken]
He isn't even afraid.

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: steady, forcing composure]
You should be.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: pleasant, chilling]
Should I.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: light, already moving on]
Close the door on your way to bed, Nurse.

--- PART 2 ---

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: calm, almost fond]
Walk with me, Nurse Kirwin.

[SFX: instrument tray clinks]

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: measured, lecturing]
Every table in this room has taught me something.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: reasonable, unhurried]
Medicine has always asked for names no one wants spoken aloud.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: quiet certainty]
The women behind that wall paid the same price surgeons have always asked.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: wistful]
Mine will simply cost less than most, in time.

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: steady, guarded]
You believe that. You actually believe that.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: unwavering]
I know it, the way I know this scalpel is sharp.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: cold, almost gentle]
History will remember my results long after your name is dust.

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: flat, controlled | speed: 0.95]
It will remember what I wrote down first.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: amused, untouched]
Paper burns, Nurse Kirwin. Results don't.

--- PART 3 ---

[DR_JONAS_WYCLIFFE | style: forcing calm]
An inspector's letter. Addressed to us both.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: unbothered, reading]
"Irregularities in ward records." How quaint.

[SFX: paper unfolding]

[DR_JONAS_WYCLIFFE | style: masking relief]
Irregularities. Not... anything worse.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: measured, amused]
Worse would require a body they could name, Jonas.

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: sharp, disbelieving]
You just told me the names. I heard you.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: cool]
You heard a nurse's word against a founding surgeon's signature.

[DR_JONAS_WYCLIFFE | style: quiet, guilty]
The board will want figures. I can... manage figures.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: dry]
Manage them the way you've managed everything else.

He signs the audit request without hesitation.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: calm, already rehearsing]
An audit only finds what I decide to leave it.

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: cold, steady]
And if it finds the ground behind the wall—

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: pleasant, final]
Then I'll simply have a very good explanation ready.
