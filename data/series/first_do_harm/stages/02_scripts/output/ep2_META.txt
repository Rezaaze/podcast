TITEL:
Ada — A Letter Marked Address Unknown

BESCHREIBUNG:
Ada holds a letter marked 'Address Unknown.' A patient discharged as recovered never reached her family's door. This is where First, Do Harm asks its cruelest question: what happens to those the hospital considers healed?

When Ada corners Wycliffe about Mrs. Hale's impossible disappearance, his denials ring hollow. Warning her that questioning the hospital's protocols could cost her everything, he offers lies dressed as compassion—the kind that make doctors sleep at night. But Ada is already asking the questions she can't unask.

In the hours after curfew, she ventures where staff aren't meant to go. Some doors open. Some don't. And some, she discovers, shouldn't be locked at all—yet something inside is breathing.

FRAGE:
How do you know when to stop trusting someone's account of what happened and start investigating for yourself?
