TITEL:
Ada — The Consent That Wasn't

BESCHREIBUNG:
Ada's fingers freeze on a patient's file: the consent signature isn't hers—it's a forgery, precise as a surgical cut. In First, Do Harm, a single moment of recognition ignites everything. Ada, who has nursed enough hands to know their every tremor, spots the deception immediately—but when she confronts Wycliffe in the theatre, his panic quickly dissolves into Moorhouse's unsettling calm. The doctor doesn't deny; he simply takes the form and leaves Ada with nothing but warmth-edged promises. That night, alone in the hospital's records, Ada searches for answers—and finds instead a terrible pattern. One signature becomes two. Two become three. The forgery isn't isolated; it's systemic, burrowed deep into the hospital's own history. And as the scale of the deception dawns on her, she realizes the real danger has only just begun.
