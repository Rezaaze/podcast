--- PART 1 ---

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: calm, almost warm]
Sit, Nurse Kirwin. I poured you a brandy.

[NARRATOR]
The clinic, after hours. Moorhouse's private office.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: measured, unhurried]
Neither of us will drink it.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: conversational, deliberate]
Dr. Wycliffe owed eleven hundred pounds in gambling debts.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: quiet, precise]
Paid in full, this January.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: cold beneath the calm]
The same month his signature started appearing beside mine.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE]
On every discharge form I signed myself.

[SFX: door hinge creaks]

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: sharp, watching Wycliffe]
Jonas.

[DR_JONAS_WYCLIFFE | style: frozen, barely audible]
I didn't— that's not—

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: pleasant, unrelenting]
Come in, Doctor. This concerns you.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: silky]
Tell her how the debt was settled.

[DR_JONAS_WYCLIFFE | style: stammering, cornered]
It isn't— it's not what you think.

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: flat, cold realization]
You never denied it.

--- PART 2 ---

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: cold, dawning realization]
Every hesitation. Every flinch. I called it conscience.

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: flat, bitter clarity]
It was only ever fear of you.

[DR_JONAS_WYCLIFFE | style: strained, trying to speak]
Ada, please, it isn't what you—

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: cutting, controlled]
Don't say my name.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: calm, observing]
You see it clearly now.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: measured, almost kind]
There's no one left in this building who'll stand beside you.

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: steady, refusing to bend]
I never needed him standing beside me.

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: quiet, resolute]
Only the truth standing behind me.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: quietly pleased]
Then you understand your position rather well.

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: deliberate, final]
I understand exactly what he's become.

[DR_JONAS_WYCLIFFE | style: desperate, low]
Ada. Look at me.

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: unmoving]
No.

--- PART 3 ---

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: soft, almost intimate]
There's a post at the Surrey house. Better pay.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: measured, persuasive]
Further from the rear wall. Quieter nights.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: calm, reasonable]
No one need speak of ledgers again.

[NURSE_ADA_KIRWIN | style: still, guarded]
That's what you gave him. Isn't it.

[DR_JONAS_WYCLIFFE | style: cracking, desperate]
Don't answer him, Ada.

[DR_JONAS_WYCLIFFE | style: raw, spilling out]
It's already done. I sent word Tuesday.

[DR_JONAS_WYCLIFFE | style: shaking]
A man outside. He has the ward count.

[DR_JONAS_WYCLIFFE | style: breaking]
It doesn't match your ledger, Elias.

[DR_ELIAS_MOORHOUSE | style: calm shattering into cold]
You did *what*.
