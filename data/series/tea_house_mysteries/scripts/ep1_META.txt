TITEL:
Master Wen — The Clock That Runs Backwards

BESCHREIBUNG:
Three rainy nights, and the old watchmaker never comes. Then his daughter stumbles through the tea house door, drenched and trembling, insisting something is terribly wrong. In this episode of The Rainy Night Tea House, Lin Qiu sets down his kettle and starts asking the questions only a former detective knows to ask. What she describes makes no sense: a workshop wiped clean of dust, tools untouched, and on the table, a single clock still ticking — running backwards. As thunder rolls over the rooftops, you will learn to talk about things that have already happened, to name the quiet dread of someone gone missing, and to sit inside the hush before a mystery reveals itself. Nothing is explained tonight. The tea grows cold, the rain does not let up, and somewhere across town, a name is about to surface: Old Shen. Listen closely. The clock is still moving.
