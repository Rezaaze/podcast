TITEL:
Reid — The Protocol Fracture

BESCHREIBUNG:
A cipher arrives for Detective Reid, built from his own closed case from years ago, and The Curator has just made this deeply personal. In The Curator's Masterwork, as a psychologist orchestrates a calculated, intimate attack on Reid's very psychology, the rules that have defined his entire career begin to fracture under enormous pressure. With Daniel Chen's public defender dismantling the department's case and the mayor's office demanding results, Reid faces an impossible choice: follow protocol and watch everything unravel, or bend the rules for the first time. Meanwhile, Mia watches from home as the man she loves becomes someone colder, more distant, increasingly unreachable.
