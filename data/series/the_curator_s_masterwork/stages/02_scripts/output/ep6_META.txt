TITEL:
Reid — Where Silence Becomes a Weapon

BESCHREIBUNG:
When a captain threatens to pull him from the investigation, a detective makes a choice that will define him forever—not whether he can catch a killer, but what he's willing to become.

Reid has always been the one who enforced the rules. Now, as institutional pressure closes in, he crosses the line he once guarded with absolute conviction. An innocent man faces arraignment on courthouse steps. The distance between Reid and Mia becomes impossible to ignore. And in his study, the Curator watches Reid's unraveling with the satisfaction of a man who has planned for this moment all along.

The Curator's Masterwork is a psychological thriller where the barrier between detective and killer dissolves one compromise at a time. This episode marks the point of no return—when ambition corrodes integrity so completely that a man becomes indistinguishable from the monster he hunts. The cost of justice has never been so personal, so irreversible.
