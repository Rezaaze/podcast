TITEL:
The Curator — The Masterwork Unveiled

BESCHREIBUNG:
The line between hunting a killer and becoming hunted blurs dangerously in The Curator's Masterwork. Reid and his partner discover a hidden gallery—a meticulously arranged shrine to a killer's obsession that reshapes the Cipher Killings case. But discovery is just the beginning. A wrongly accused suspect gains powerful allies in the press, the department faces political pressure, and Reid's marriage fractures under the weight of an investigation that has literally invaded his home. When a new coded message appears directly referencing the gallery, Reid is drawn back to the scene alone, unable to resist the pull. This episode charts the moment when a case stops being about solving a crime and starts being about a detective's slow dissolution—the realization that some obsessions can never be shared, only endured.
