TITEL:
The Puppet Strings — When He Sees He's Been Played

BESCHREIBUNG:
Reid has spent every episode convinced he's directing the hunt. Tonight, he discovers he may have been following a map drawn long before he knew the game existed. As the pieces finally align—each thread leading not forward, but back—he confronts the possibility that the Curator's patience was the only real strategy all along. The investigation explodes into three colliding crises: Park's methodical indictment of Reid's shortcuts, Mia's quiet decision to leave, and a formal inquiry that transforms him from detective to suspect. The Curator's Masterwork faces its central tension head-on: the marriage can't survive what the case demands, and the case can't survive what Reid has become. Somewhere in the dark, a patient hand makes one final adjustment and waits for Reid to answer.
