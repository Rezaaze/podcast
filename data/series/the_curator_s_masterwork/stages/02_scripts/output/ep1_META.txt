TITEL:
Reid — When the Killer Calls Your Name

BESCHREIBUNG:
A killer writes to Detective Marcus Reid by name, launching a twisted game. A staged body surfaces at an abandoned mill, a cipher note pinned above it—proof the killer has been watching far longer than anyone realizes. Reid becomes consumed by the puzzle, determined to decode the opening move of this deadly correspondence. But as he chases the killer through ciphers and dead drops, his marriage crumbles silently at home. His wife Mia waits for an anniversary dinner that never comes. His partner Park watches him slip away into obsession. In The Curator's Masterwork, a psychological thriller that blends prestige crime with domestic devastation, the hunt for a serial killer and the unraveling of a detective's life become inseparable. The game has begun—and Reid doesn't yet understand the true cost of playing.
