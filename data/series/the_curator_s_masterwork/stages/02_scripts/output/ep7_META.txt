TITEL:
Reid Elliot — When the Mirror Stares Back

BESCHREIBUNG:
Detective Reid Elliot is cracking, and everyone around him knows it. As he descends into the Curator's latest cipher, his analysis begins to mirror the killer's own voice—a transformation that terrifies both his partner and his wife. But the real horror emerges when a new dead drop reveals something impossible: the Curator has been studying Reid with surgical precision, knowing secrets from his past that exist nowhere in any file. In The Curator's Masterwork, the investigation becomes personal and perilous as Reid grapples with the question that haunts every hunter: can you pursue a predator without becoming one? With his marriage fracturing, his team watching him unravel, and a killer proving he knows his quarry better than anyone, Reid faces the realization that the cat-and-mouse game was rigged from the start.
