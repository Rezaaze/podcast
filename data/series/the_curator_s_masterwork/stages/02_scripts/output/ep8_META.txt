TITEL:
Reid — When Silence Becomes Guilt

BESCHREIBUNG:
A lab report proves Daniel Chen's innocence — and Detective Reid chooses to hide it. What begins as temporary hesitation becomes calculated strategy, then lie, then complicity. As an innocent man accepts guilt to escape a system he can't beat, as Reid's marriage fractures under mounting deceptions, and as the Curator's latest tableau reveals a knowing that pierces too deep, Reid faces a truth: the distance between hunter and hunted is far narrower than he believed.

The Curator's Masterwork explores corruption and moral collapse through a detective becoming indistinguishable from the killer he hunts. A psychological drama where justice breaks from within, where innocence becomes dangerous, and where one choice shatters everyone it touches.
