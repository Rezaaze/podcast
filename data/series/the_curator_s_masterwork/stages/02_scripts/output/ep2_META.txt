TITEL:
Reid — The Wrong Certainty

BESCHREIBUNG:
An arrest. A torn receipt bearing Daniel Chen's thumbprint. Relief spreads through the department—the mayor's office unburdened, the team unburdened, Detective Reid unburdened—though not for long. As evidence accumulates around an innocent man, Reid's certainty begins to fracture. In The Curator's Masterwork, an arrest doesn't solve the mystery; it creates new ones. Reid's marriage strains as his obsession deepens. His partnership with Park fractures as doubt takes root. Joy Chen fights publicly for her brother's innocence while the precinct marches forward with conviction. Then a new cipher arrives—one that challenges everything Reid believes about the case. The evidence starts to feel wrong. The timeline doesn't hold. And Detective Reid begins to wonder whether they've caught the killer or simply the convenient suspect.
