Vince Lo — Quiet Leverage

BESCHREIBUNG:
In the harbor's quietest hour, three separate pressures tighten beneath the surface. Vince Lo moves with calculated care, quietly positioning his challenge to the Chens' salvage dominance. Mei faces the hard deadline of a balloon payment that threatens everything she's built—borrowed time running out. Danny and Kai deepen their secret rather than risk exposure, their connection becoming both their greatest refuge and their most dangerous vulnerability. The Meridian Line explores what happens when ambition, financial desperation, and forbidden intimacy converge under a single calm surface. Watch as individual pressures build in parallel, each person isolated in their own crisis, unaware of the storms gathering around them. This is the moment before the tide turns—when every choice feels irreversible and the stakes have never been higher.
