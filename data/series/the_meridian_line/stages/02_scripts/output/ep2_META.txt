The Meridian Star hearing brings every hidden pressure to a head. Vince fights desperately for the Chens, but even victory tastes like reprieve—temporary safety rather than resolution. The Chambers fallout deepens workplace divisions. Frank discovers evidence of Mei's carefully hidden financial world, uncovering a secret that doesn't completely expose her but fundamentally changes what lies between them. And in a single, breathless moment, Danny and Kai come within seconds of being seen together—just as their companies are locked in public opposition.

The Meridian Line is about what families survive and what we're willing to pay for that survival. In this episode, four separate pressures break through the surface at once. No one was prepared for what happens when they all converge.
