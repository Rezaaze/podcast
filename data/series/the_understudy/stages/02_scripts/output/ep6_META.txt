TITEL:
Elias — The Certainty That Blinds

BESCHREIBUNG:
A clue surfaces that could expose everything—but only if someone looks at it with fresh eyes. Elias has a theory that fits perfectly, and perfect fits leave no room for doubt. As pressure intensifies from above and hidden alliances crack under the weight of scrutiny, the investigation circles closer to the truth without quite confronting it. In The Understudy, investigation is as much about blindness as sight: what we choose to see, what our convictions prevent us from noticing, and how dangerously close we can drift to catastrophe while mistaking certainty for clarity. This episode explores the fault lines where personal conviction becomes an investigator's greatest liability—where the very evidence you trust most becomes the instrument of your undoing.
