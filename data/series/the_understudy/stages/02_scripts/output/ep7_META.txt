TITEL:
Elias — What Was in the Coat Pocket

BESCHREIBUNG:
A raid on Han's grocery. False charges. An arrest that sends shockwaves through the precinct, into Elias Vance's carefully managed world, and into the fractured heart of his marriage. For the department, it's a frame that raises dangerous questions. For Elias, it's the moment when his own actions demand to be paid for. For Mira, it's the beginning of understanding what her husband has become. In The Understudy, institutional corruption collides with domestic betrayal. Han becomes a pawn in a game orchestrated by forces beyond his knowledge. Loyalty fractures under pressure. Trust becomes a weapon. And when the woman beside you becomes a stranger, there's nowhere left to hide. This is the night the city learns what its own machinery is capable of. This is the night three separate worlds align. This is the moment everything changes.
