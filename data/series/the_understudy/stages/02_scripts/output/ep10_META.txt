TITEL:
Elias Vance — What Was Built to Break

BESCHREIBUNG:
A detective walks into the fog certain he finally understands the truth waiting for him there—but the real design reveals itself only when it's too late to turn back. The Understudy's season finale follows Elias Vance through a violent night at the docks and into the quiet aftermath that destroys what violence cannot touch. Charges collapse. His reputation remains fractured. His wife sees him transformed in ways no apology can fix. His partner discovers the timeline doesn't match. One truth emerges with absolute clarity: Elias was engineered from the beginning to become exactly what the killer wanted—a man walking a blurred line between certainty and doubt, between duty and complicity, forever questioning his own actions. Nothing else resolves. Nothing else clarifies. The masterwork stands complete.
