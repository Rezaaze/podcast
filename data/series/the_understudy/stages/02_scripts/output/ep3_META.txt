TITEL:
Elias — When Certainty Comes Easy

BESCHREIBUNG:
A matchbook and a receipt are found at the scene, and they point not to a culprit but to a direction Detective Elias is eager to follow. In The Understudy, suspicion blooms from circumstance, and momentum becomes certainty. His partner Lin harbors doubts; the evidence is threadbare. But Elias feels the pull of a lead he can finally close, and doubt is dangerous to his need for action.

At the docks, evidence is re-examined and suspicions are suppressed. At home, Mira finds a note she shouldn't have and makes a choice. At the precinct, a captain demands the full file. At a rare books store, something is carefully put away. And at Han's grocery store, a man prepares for what's coming—a line of questioning that has already convicted him before any question is asked.

This is how frames are built: one thin thread at a time, one decision at a time, until innocence becomes irrelevant.
