TITEL:
Elias Vance — The Hidden Game

BESCHREIBUNG:
When Detective Elias Vance cracks the killer's hidden message, he makes a choice that will alter everything: he keeps it to himself. As The Puppeteer's game spirals deeper, Elias becomes consumed by coded breadcrumbs leading him through rare bookshops and abandoned docks — a hunt he conducts in secret. But obsession has a cost. His wife Mira has noticed the late nights, the closeted conversations, the walls he's building between them. And his partner Lin senses the gaps in his story, watching him carefully for signs of something she can't quite name. When a mysterious note appears — one written in a hand that isn't his own — Elias finds himself at a crossroads between personal obsession and professional duty. The Understudy examines what we hide from those closest to us, and the price of keeping score in a game meant for one.
