TITEL:
Elias — Where the Silence Finally Ends

BESCHREIBUNG:
A signal leads Detective Chen toward a name tangled in the darkest corners of his case file. Across the city, an old man arrives at a building with paperwork he's kept for decades, carrying years of silence he can no longer hold. In The Long Fare's most exposing hour yet, the careful distance between people finally collapses. Codes give up their secrets. Old stories demand to be heard. And the kind of truth that requires a decade to finally voice doesn't announce itself gently—it shatters the careful barriers that have kept people safe from each other. By the time this episode ends, three characters will know something that fundamentally changes everything they believed about what happened, about who bears responsibility, and about the true cost of silence when someone you love is drowning in it.
