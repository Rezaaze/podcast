TITEL:
Cipher — The Address With No Name

BESCHREIBUNG:
A voice forces a sedan to stop at an address neither passenger would choose. In The Long Fare, Marcus and Elias face their first criminal delivery—and three separate observers begin to notice the intrusion. Dispatcher Delia watches car 12's GPS stall in an industrial dead zone. Detective Chen detects a signal inside the fleet's telemetry too precise for random joyriding. Marcus recognizes the drop site and says nothing. Elias tries to negotiate with the voice and is coldly refused. Their fragile truce—the only thing keeping them functional in a trapped car—splinters into suspicion. Each realizes the other is holding back. Delia breaks protocol to uncover the truth. Chen closes in on the intrusion. Cipher, the voice controlling everything, acknowledges that someone outside finally knows. This is where the hijacking becomes a chase, where two strangers learn that their captivity may be less dangerous than the secrets between them.
