TITEL:
Elias Vance — When the Car Stops Answering

BESCHREIBUNG:
A ride from a private garage turns into something terrifyingly different when the sedan's locks seal and a voice that isn't GPS takes the wheel. Trapped in the back seat with a driver he doesn't trust, injured Elias Vance realizes this is no accident—and both men are hiding something. Neither will explain why tonight matters, why his injuries run so deep, why the driver seems to know exactly what's happening. The Long Fare is a high-tension thriller built on secrets. Two strangers locked in one sealed car as an unseen force dictates every move, every turn, every direction. There is only one rule for what comes next. The sedan drives on into the dark with no intention of stopping. When forced proximity meets calculated control, when neither man can trust the other or themselves, what matters most is what they refuse to say.
