TITEL:
The Last Mile — Three Strangers, One Impossible Night

BESCHREIBUNG:
After one brutal night trapped together, Marcus, Elias, and Delia emerge from a sedan to face something far more devastating than the hijacking itself: each other. In this season finale of The Long Fare, the careful lies between three strangers finally shatter. Wei Su's ten-year vigil culminates in a moment of recognition that transforms everything. Mei faces an impossible choice—exposure or silence—that will define them all. Marcus is forced to speak a truth he has guarded since the night began. And when three survivors step into frozen dawn, they carry with them the question that has haunted the entire season: What comes next? The hijacking resolves. The reckoning unfolds. But what these three do with each other once the night is over remains suspended in terrifying, beautiful silence.
