TITEL:
Marcus — When the Stranger Becomes Necessary

BESCHREIBUNG:
A task with a clock forces two strangers into an alliance neither of them wanted. Marcus, the driver, has survived on control and the secrets he keeps locked away; Elias, the passenger, on pride and the careful distance between trust and betrayal. In The Long Fare, cooperation becomes the only weapon left, and it demands a price neither man anticipated. Delia watches from a distant room, her concern for Marcus beginning to look like something more than protocol—something her supervisors are starting to notice. As Marcus and Elias execute a plan that demands genuine vulnerability, they cross a line neither can uncross: the stranger beside you might matter more than the secrets you've kept all your life. What happens when two men learn to trust each other will change everything that comes next.
