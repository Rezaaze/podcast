--- SCENE 1 ---

3. Marcus reads the numbers back with forced calm, testing whether Cipher will fill the silence; she doesn't.

2. Streetlights thin out as the car moves toward a darker, more industrial stretch of the city.
3. Marcus notes the direction under his breath, a driver's habit, without yet naming why it unsettles him.
4. Elias watches the changing scenery with open unease, out of his depth outside his usual streets.
5. The audience senses the car is closing in on ground that means something neither passenger has said aloud.

--- SCENE 3 ---
1. Cipher orders the trunk unlocked and instructs Marcus, specifically, to retrieve a sealed package from inside.
2. Marcus complies under the dashboard's watch, finding a plain, heavy box with no markings he recognizes.
3. Elias watches from the back seat without offering to help, his silence more calculated than passive.
4. Elias's eyes catch on something about the package or the site that clearly means more to him than he lets on, but he says nothing.
5. Marcus sets the box inside the car as instructed, unaware of what it actually is or why it matters.
6. Cipher gives no explanation for the box, only confirmation that it now travels with them.

--- SCENE 4 ---

--- SCENE 5 ---

4. Elias's composure slips for a moment, unused to being refused by anyone, let alone something he can't see.
5. Marcus watches the exchange, filing away that Elias's instinct under pressure is to buy his way out — a habit that tells its own story.

--- SCENE 7 ---
1. Marcus's mind keeps circling back to the cross streets of the site they just left, certain he's driven near them before.
2. A specific, unwanted memory tries to surface, and he pushes it back down before it fully forms.
3. His hands tighten slightly on the wheel, a private reaction Elias notices but can't place.
4. Elias asks, carefully, if something about the last stop is bothering him.
5. Marcus deflects with a flat, practiced answer, giving away nothing of what the address actually means to him.

--- SCENE 8 ---

4. He pulls the raw signal data himself rather than waiting on a junior analyst's summary.

--- SCENE 9 ---
1. Chen traces the injection's method and finds a level of sophistication far beyond typical joyriding or smash-and-grab ransomware.
2. He rules out the usual suspects his unit normally chases, unsettled by how clean and deliberate the intrusion looks.
