TITEL:
Elias — The Warehouse Reckoning

BESCHREIBUNG:
At a ruined warehouse on the edge of the city, three strangers finally converge—and each discovers that nothing about this night was coincidence. In The Long Fare, a voice has choreographed every choice, every turn, every mile of this terrible journey. Tonight, the voice becomes flesh, and buried secrets explode into the open. A hijacking. A reckoning. A man who engineered the collision of three lives. The driver, the passenger, and the mysterious orchestrator must now stand together in the wreckage and face what each of them has been hiding. Revelations unspool through steel and shadow: truths about The Harrow Reckoning, about choices made and lies buried, about a letter burned and a man who never really left. As the real stakes surface, each of them must decide whether survival means telling the truth—or walking away from the fence forever.
