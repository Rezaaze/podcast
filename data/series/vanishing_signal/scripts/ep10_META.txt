TITEL:
Mei Voss — The Vanishing Act

BESCHREIBUNG:
The money moves at dawn, and so does she. In this finale of Vanishing Signal, Mei steps out of the shadows she built her disappearance from, meeting the people she used one final time before the truth catches up to all of them. Jason gets his last conversation with a woman he thought he'd lost. Her father hears a voice he'd stopped hoping for. David races toward the marina chasing a ghost who might still be standing on the dock. Dana can't decide if she's there for the story or for him, and that choice may already be out of her hands. Meanwhile, Marcus counts what's missing, and Elena moves quietly to claim what's left of Voss Dynamics before anyone else understands what happened. Every relationship in this season converges on one dock, one account, one morning. Nobody walks away holding the same piece of the truth.
