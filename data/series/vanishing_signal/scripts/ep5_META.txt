TITEL:
The Accusation — David Hears What His Words Became

BESCHREIBUNG:
The episode drops before dawn and doesn't stop spreading. Marcus wakes to a flooded inbox and a stock chart already bleeding red, the first casualty of an accusation he never saw coming. Elena reads the transcript twice, not with panic but with the cold precision of someone calculating angles. Dana, meanwhile, basks in the fallout on camera, treating detonation as triumph. Jason spirals in private, convinced his own exposure is only a matter of time. When Marcus turns his suspicion on Elena, she meets him with a denial so clean it leaves him doubting his own instincts rather than hers. Across town, David sits alone at the Lin house and listens to the whole thing from the beginning, piecing together exactly what has been built from what he once said in confidence. Vanishing Signal turns the volume up on trust, ambition, and the particular damage caused by people who know precisely which truths to leave out.
