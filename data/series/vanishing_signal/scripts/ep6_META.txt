David — The Trust That Cracked First

BESCHREIBUNG:
Two days of silence can say more than any conversation. David has stopped answering Dana's calls, and when he finally shows up at the Lin house, it isn't for comfort — it's for answers. What she used from his grief, and how far she let it go, sits between them like a door that won't fully open. Meanwhile, inside the company he still trusts, Elena is quietly rebuilding her power one lunch at a time, and Marcus scrambles to hold his allies together before the vote that could decide everything. And Jason, worn thin by something he can no longer carry alone, makes a choice that pulls him toward Dana's studio instead of away from it. Vanishing Signal follows what happens when private pain becomes shared material, and the people closest to it start keeping their own scores. This episode, Cracks, is about the moment trust stops being assumed and starts being tested.
