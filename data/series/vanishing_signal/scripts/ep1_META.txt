TITEL:
Mei Lin — The Room Nobody Slept In

BESCHREIBUNG:
A dorm bed still made. A phone that won't stop ringing. A city that smells a story before it smells grief. When Mei Lin vanishes without a trace, the people around her reveal themselves in the space of a single night: a father who can't yet form the words, a fiancé whose statement sounds rehearsed before it should, and a reporter who treats disappearance as opportunity long before anyone calls it a crime. In the quiet corners — a locked lab terminal, a dinner table too calm to be real, a phone call made behind a closed door — small choices harden into consequences no one can undo. Vanishing Signal opens its first chapter here, in the hours when instinct outruns evidence, and everyone with something to protect starts protecting it. By the time a file lands in an inbox at three in the morning, the story has already chosen its shape — and its casualties.
