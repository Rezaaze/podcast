TITEL:
Marcus Voss — The Vote That Broke the Room

BESCHREIBUNG:
The boardroom at Voss Tower fills with tension long before the first ballot is cast. Jason finally gives Dana a piece of the truth about the clips — enough to reshape everything she thought she understood, though not enough to close the case. As the confidence vote looms, Marcus makes his final, strained appeal to hold the company together, while Elena answers with a counter-case so calm and precise it changes the outcome before the count is even read. When the votes are tallied, control slips from Marcus's hands for the first time. But the real reckoning comes after, when the room empties and Elena finds him alone. What she says next has been sitting between them for weeks, unspoken. Vanishing Signal returns with a boardroom drama that trades gunfire for quiet devastation — where the sharpest weapon in the room is simply knowing something first.
