TITEL:
Dana — The Name She Finally Said Out Loud

BESCHREIBUNG:
A phone call nobody can explain. A name nobody dares to say. In this episode of Vanishing Signal, Dana stops circling the truth and walks straight at Jason, demanding answers he's spent months burying. What comes out of him changes everything: Mei never left, she never stopped watching, and every move on the board has been hers. While the confrontation lands, Marcus is elsewhere, quietly setting numbers in motion that no one at the company will see coming until it's too late. Elena stumbles onto paperwork that shouldn't exist yet, and David, shaken by a call he can't make sense of, turns to the one person he trusts to help him understand it. Dana has the story now, whole and undeniable, and the equipment to capture it sitting right in front of her. What she does with it, and with him, is the question the episode leaves hanging in the air.
