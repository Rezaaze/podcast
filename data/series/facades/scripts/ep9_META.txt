TITEL:
Victoria — The Night She Chose Her Son Over Justice

BESCHREIBUNG:
Some mothers protect their children. Victoria rebuilds the world around hers. In this episode of Facades, she finally asks Konrad the question she has been circling for a year — what really happened at the boathouse the night Elena vanished — and gets an answer that should end everything. Instead, it becomes the beginning of something colder. As Konrad's confession unravels the affair, the lie, and the year of silence that followed, Victoria doesn't reach for the phone. She reaches for a plan. Money moves. Debts vanish. A son's future is quietly, surgically rearranged, even as an anonymous blackmailer edges closer to the truth Victoria is working so hard to bury. This is a portrait of maternal loyalty stripped of sentiment — precise, unnerving, and entirely without mercy. Listen closely: the family's calm has never been more dangerous than it is right now.
