TITEL:
Konrad — Whatever It Takes

BESCHREIBUNG:
Some debts can't be paid with money alone. In this episode of Facades, Konrad's lender stops issuing warnings and starts issuing deadlines, and the second forged signature comes easier than the first — a small mercy he grants himself, and the last one he can afford. Across town, Marcus finally hands Chloe the memory he's been carrying alone: an argument by the boathouse, weeks before Elena disappeared, that he's spent every day since trying to unremember. And in a quiet office full of ledgers no one reads twice, Victoria finds a signature that doesn't belong to the story she's been told. A second note arrives, sharper than the first, meant for Konrad alone. Then the family sits down to dinner, because that's what families do — smile, pass the wine, and say nothing true at all.
