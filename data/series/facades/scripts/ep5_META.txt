TITEL:
Elena Marsh — What the Report Doesn't Forgive

BESCHREIBUNG:
A phone call at dawn undoes months of careful composure, and Julian answers it before anyone else in the house is even awake. The medical examiner's office has one more thing to say about Elena — something that turns grief into an entirely different kind of reckoning. As Julian carries the news through the family, each person receives it differently: Victoria's control finally cracks, Marcus reads it through the lens of a fight he witnessed and wishes he hadn't, and Chloe holds it in silence, turning it over like a stone she can't put down. Meanwhile, a new demand arrives from someone who understands exactly how vulnerable this family has become. At the dinner table, one man says almost nothing at all — and no one thinks to ask why. This episode of Facades is about the moment a family realizes the story they told themselves about Elena's death was never the whole story.
