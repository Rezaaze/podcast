TITEL:
Victoria Vanderburg — The Price of a Quiet Legacy

BESCHREIBUNG:
Some victories are won in silence, at midnight, over paperwork nobody will ever read aloud. In this Facades finale, Victoria closes every door she opened this season, and closes them the way she does everything: quietly, completely, without a single wasted word. A brother disappears under a name that isn't his. A blackmailer is bought instead of burned, given a chair instead of an exposure. A younger brother is handed a story he almost, almost refuses to believe. And a daughter stands where a door used to be, choosing the family she was born into over the one she almost chose instead. Nothing here is loud. Nothing here needs to be. By the time the lake goes still outside the study window, the Vanderburg name is intact, the story the world will tell is set, and only one person in this house knows the full price of what that costs — and she intends to carry it alone. The house always wins. It just doesn't tell you what it keeps.
