TITEL:
Victoria Marsh — Cracks in the Marble

BESCHREIBUNG:
The police don't knock politely forever, and the moment they walk onto the estate grounds, every carefully rehearsed answer in this house starts to sound like a lie. Detectives move through the foyer with quiet, procedural patience, circling back to a scar Konrad has explained away a hundred times, but never to anyone with a badge. Upstairs, Victoria closes her study door and does what she does best: makes a problem disappear without raising her voice, wiring away a debt no one else is supposed to know exists. Downstairs, Chloe hangs up half a second too late, and Richard's silence says more than any accusation could. And outside the gates, Julian finally says the name this family has spent a year trying not to say out loud. This episode of Facades watches composure fracture in real time, one controlled gesture at a time, as everyone insists nothing is wrong at all.
