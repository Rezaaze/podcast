TITEL:
Isabelle Vanderburg — The Audit That Reads Like a Confession

BESCHREIBUNG:
Trust, in this family, was never free — and Isabelle just found a way to charge for hers. When she offers to conduct a quiet internal audit of the Vanderburg trust, Victoria accepts out of sheer exhaustion, unaware she's just handed her most careful adversary the keys to everything. Alone with the original documents, Victoria finally lets herself see what she's been avoiding: two signatures, one truth she can no longer unknow. Outside the gates, an unfamiliar face draws suspicion Konrad can't quite explain away, and inside her own conflicted silence, Chloe chooses not to tell Julian what Marcus saw — a decision she won't examine too closely. Facades keeps circling the same unbearable question: who in this house is actually protecting anyone, and who's just protecting themselves. Composure has never looked so much like confession.
