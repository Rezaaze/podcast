TITEL:
Victoria — Everything She Now Knows

BESCHREIBUNG:
A single lie can hold for years — until the paperwork surfaces. In this episode of Facades, Julian's discovery of a buried land contract forces Konrad into a lie he can't sustain, even as Fong's enforcers step out of the shadows and into daylight with a warning that leaves no room for another delay. Meanwhile, Chloe finally hears the question she's been dreading from Marcus, and finds she still can't answer it. But the episode's quiet center belongs to Victoria, who corners Marcus with patience instead of anger and finally hears the truth about the boathouse — a truth she spends the rest of the night arranging into a shape she can no longer refuse to see. And somewhere beyond the estate walls, a fifth note arrives, sharper than the ones before it, forcing the family's oldest question into the open: how much longer can the name hold?
