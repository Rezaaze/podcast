TITEL:
Victoria — The First Lie She Ever Caught Him In

BESCHREIBUNG:
Some doors only creak open once, and this is the episode where the family stops trusting the walls of their own house. The blackmailer's fourth note arrives bolder than the last, no longer whispered to one person but slapped down where everyone can read it — and suspicion turns inward fast, ugly, and personal. Julian's accusation lands in the wrong place but far too close to the truth for comfort. Meanwhile Victoria, holding proof she can no longer explain away, finally says Konrad's name out loud to his face — and watches him lie to her for what she now realizes might not be the first time. Elsewhere, a single photograph threatens to unravel a secret no one was supposed to be looking for. In Facades, the smallest gestures carry the heaviest weight, and in this episode, a look across a crowded room says more than any confession could.
