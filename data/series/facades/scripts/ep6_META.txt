TITEL:
Victoria — The Threads Only She Can See

BESCHREIBUNG:
A single phone call rewrites everything, and no one in this family is prepared for what it means. When the paternity result comes back and the child isn't Julian's, the news doesn't explode — it seeps, quietly reshaping every glance across the dinner table. Julian, desperate for an answer that keeps his world intact, begins compiling a private list of suspects, certain the truth lives outside these walls. Meanwhile Victoria, saying nothing, starts requesting records she has no real reason to need, chasing a suspicion she can't yet name aloud. Outside the estate, an old debt resurfaces with a warning that can't be ignored twice, and inside it, a secret nearly slips free before being swallowed back down. This episode of Facades moves through hushed hallways and unfinished sentences, where every character is protecting something and no one is protecting the same thing. What looks like grief is also strategy — and someone here already knows more than they're saying.
