TITEL:
Victoria Vanderburg — A Hundred Years, One Body in the Lake

BESCHREIBUNG:
Five hundred guests raise their glasses to a century of the Vanderburg name — and none of them notice the crew working the lakeshore in the dark. Victoria has built tonight to be flawless: the toasts, the lighting, the legacy, all curated to the last champagne flute. But beneath the pool house, something surfaces that isn't a pipe, and the news reaches her before it reaches the police. While she performs the gracious host, her children are performing their own quiet betrayals — a hallway call slipped in mid-toast, a terrace promise made in a currency that doesn't exist. By the time the last car pulls away, the gala is over and the real evening begins, as Victoria pulls her children into the study to tell them what the lake gave up. What waits for each of them afterward, in an identical envelope on an unmarked pillow, is only the beginning. This episode of Facades asks what a family will protect when the truth finally comes ashore.
