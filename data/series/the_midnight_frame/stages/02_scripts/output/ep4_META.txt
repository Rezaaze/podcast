TITEL:
Hae — The Hours Off Record

BESCHREIBUNG:
In The Midnight Frame, a night off the clock becomes a rare moment of unguarded intimacy. As professional walls come down in domestic spaces and quiet corners of the precinct, the characters navigate what stays hidden and what can't remain unsaid. Scrambled eggs and early mornings. A text in the middle of a shift. Two people learning to trust each other beyond the work, and two others discovering an unexpected rapport. Quiet scenes build to something deeper—lingering glances, conversations that shouldn't be possible, the dangerous ease of people who know too much about each other. Before everything changes, we sit in these moments: in the spaces between confession and silence, in the fragile peace of people who've chosen to let their guard down.
