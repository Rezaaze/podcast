TITEL:
Sun — When the Story Breaks, So Does the Silence

BESCHREIBUNG:
The article is live, and the bullpen cannot unhear it. In this episode of Chain of Custody, Zoe Han's first exposé on precinct financial irregularities forces Captain Sun into frantic damage control, working the phones and denying everything to anyone who will listen. But the ripples spread further than he can manage: Voss recalibrates his leverage over Mei Lin the moment he learns the article exists, Kai Yang starts connecting his captain's panic to the story itself, and Anna Wu recognizes a name from her missing husband's own notes. As two investigations quietly merge and trust deepens where it shouldn't, the walls tighten around a man used to controlling every version of the truth. Off the Record traces the moment a cover-up stops being containable and starts becoming a countdown, told through the people closest to the pressure and the ones just beginning to notice it.
