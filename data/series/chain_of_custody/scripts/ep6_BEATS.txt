--- SCENE 1 ---
1. Zoe Han's article on precinct financial irregularities goes live, naming discretionary fund discrepancies without yet naming Sun outright.
2. The bullpen falls silent as officers read it on their phones, then erupts into whispered speculation.
3. Kai Yang watches reactions carefully, noting who looks worried and who looks merely curious.
4. Mei Lin skims it distractedly, her mind still on Voss and the confession she can't take back.
5. The audience recognizes this as the spark that will force every character's hidden thread into motion at once.

--- SCENE 2 ---
1. Sun works the phones, calling contacts and reporters, denying any irregularities in categorical terms.
2. He keeps his tone controlled in front of the precinct, projecting confidence he doesn't feel.
3. Privately, he calculates how much of the discretionary fund trail Zoe could actually trace.
4. He decides denial and pressure on his own contacts can still hold the line, misjudging how much ground he's already lost.
5. The audience sees the gap between his outward composure and his real, escalating panic.

--- SCENE 3 ---
1. Mei Lin mentions the article to Voss during a routine exchange, not realizing its significance to him.
2. Voss listens and immediately understands Sun's network is destabilizing under the exposure.
3. He conceals his own recalculation from Mei Lin, letting her think he's simply making conversation.
4. He recognizes this as an opening to accelerate his plans before Sun's cover-up unravels around him too.
5. The audience sees Voss quietly repricing his leverage over both Mei Lin and Sun in the same moment.

--- SCENE 4 ---
1. Kai Yang notices Sun's unusually tense phone calls and clipped answers throughout the day.
2. He recalls the tampered log fragment he already found and starts turning over a new possibility.
3. He wonders, for the first time, whether Sun's panic over the article and the lock-in cover-up are connected.
4. He says nothing to anyone, choosing to keep testing the theory privately before acting.
5. The audience watches him move one step closer to a truth he doesn't yet have full proof of.

--- SCENE 5 ---
1. Anna Wu reads Zoe's published article on her own, scanning it for anything relevant to Daniel.
2. She recognizes a name in the piece that matches one scrawled in Daniel's private notes.
3. She feels a jolt of vindication — proof she isn't imagining a connection the precinct dismissed.
4. She resolves to bring this directly to Zoe rather than sit on it alone.
5. The audience sees the first hard link between Daniel's disappearance and Sun's corruption crystallize in print.

--- SCENE 6 ---
1. Voss offers Mei Lin a genuine, specific fragment of Daniel's location, more concrete than any prior detail.
2. He frames the exchange as requiring something back from her, a real concession rather than mere conversation.
3. Mei Lin hesitates only briefly, weighing the request against how close she feels to finding Daniel.
4. She agrees to the concession, believing this proves Voss is finally cooperating in good faith.
5. Voss privately notes her agreement as the next stage of his leverage over her, not genuine trust.
6. The audience understands this fragment is real but incomplete, and that Mei Lin has just moved deeper into his design.

--- SCENE 7 ---
1. Sun meets his harbor contact urgently, away from any precinct eyes.
2. The contact warns him the network is feeling exposed and pressure is mounting from multiple directions.
3. Sun insists he can still manage the fallout if everyone stays quiet and disciplined.
4. He doesn't yet know Anna and Zoe are actively comparing notes, only that Kai Yang and the article are threats.
5. The audience sees Sun still fighting the wrong battle while the real one closes in from another side.

--- SCENE 8 ---
1. Zoe Han and Anna Wu meet again, this time explicitly agreeing to combine what they know.
2. Anna lays out everything from the Blue Lantern and Daniel's notes; Zoe shares more of her financial trail than before.
3. Together they realize the name from the article and the name from Daniel's notes are the same person.
4. Zoe still holds back her strongest suspicion about Sun's direct involvement, wary of tipping him off too soon.
5. Anna, driven by urgency for her brother, presses Zoe to move faster than Zoe thinks is safe.
6. The audience sees the alliance formalize, tightening the noose around Sun without his knowledge.

--- SCENE 9 ---
1. Mei Lin reflects on the fragment Voss gave her, feeling closer to finding Daniel than she has in weeks.
2. She starts extending Voss small, informal trust — a softer tone, more candor than procedure allows.
3. She tells herself this is calculated strategy, not softness, even as the line blurs.
4. She doesn't recognize her own dependence on him growing with every exchange.
5. The audience sees her replicate, almost exactly, the dynamic Voss engineered around the Okafor confession.

--- SCENE 10 ---
1. Kai Yang follows the location fragment on his own time, cross-referencing it against known safehouse patterns.
2. He gets close enough to realize the trail intersects with irregularities around the interrogation room's own records.
3. He doesn't yet have the final connection, but senses the two mysteries — Daniel and the lock-in — are tangled together.
4. He decides this is too sensitive to bring to Sun and keeps building his case alone.
5. The audience sees him unknowingly approaching the exact secret Sun is most desperate to bury.

--- SCENE 11 ---
1. Sun receives word, through a nervous intermediary, that Zoe Han and Anna Wu have been seen together multiple times.
2. He realizes the two women are actively pooling information against him, not working separate angles.
3. His carefully maintained sense of control cracks, his panic now harder to hide even from himself.
4. He understands his window to contain this quietly is closing far faster than he assumed.
5. The audience watches his blind spot collapse all at once, forcing him toward more desperate action.

--- SCENE 12 ---
1. Voss senses Mei Lin's compliance has deepened enough to ask for more than information this time.
2. He raises a new, far more dangerous request — something that would expose her far beyond the lock-in itself.
3. He frames it carefully, as if it were simply the next natural step in their arrangement.
4. Mei Lin recognizes this ask is different, bigger, and hesitates in a way she hasn't before.
5. Voss reads her hesitation and adjusts his approach, patient rather than pushing too hard too fast.
6. The audience recognizes this as the moment Voss's true endgame — his own release — finally comes into view.

--- SCENE 13 ---
1. Cornered by the article, Kai Yang's suspicion, and the Zoe-Anna alliance, Sun feels every side closing in at once.
2. He weighs his dwindling options and concludes a passive strategy of denial is no longer enough.
3. He makes a decision to act drastically, though the specifics of his plan remain unspoken even to himself.
4. He tells no one at the precinct, isolating the decision entirely within his own panic.
5. The audience senses this decision will collide violently with Mei Lin's lock-in, Kai Yang's investigation, and Voss's own plans, without yet knowing exactly how.
