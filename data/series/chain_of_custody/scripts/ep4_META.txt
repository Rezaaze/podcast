TITEL:
Mei Lin — The Room the Log Forgot

BESCHREIBUNG:
A missing sign-in sheet. A silence that stretches too long. In this episode of Chain of Custody, Captain Sun moves fast and quiet to erase every trace that Mei Lin was ever in that interrogation room, while Voss circles closer with a different kind of offer — not a threat, but the promise that he's the only one telling her the truth. As Kai Yang starts pulling at a gap in the digital log and Zoe Han chases numbers that don't add up at the Blue Lantern, two separate investigations edge toward the same buried fund, the same harbor meeting, the same captain. Anna Wu waits by a phone that won't ring with answers. Mei Lin, caught between a system built to protect itself and a man who claims to see through it, has to decide who she can still trust — and how much of herself she's willing to lose finding out. Loyalty, corruption, and the cost of silence collide in a precinct where the paperwork lies better than the people do.
