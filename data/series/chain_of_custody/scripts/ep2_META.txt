TITEL:
Mei Lin — The Door Locks

BESCHREIBUNG:
A door closes, and everything changes. Mei Lin seals herself alone in the room with Voss, certain she can extract what she needs on her own terms — but Voss has never been the one who answers questions, only the one who waits for them to answer themselves. Outside, Kai Yang notices the silence first: a missed check-in, an interrogation log gone quiet, a lie through the door that doesn't quite hold. He starts asking his own questions, and eight years back, he finds a file with her name on it. Inside, Voss drops a single word into the stillness like a stone into water, then lets that silence do the rest of the talking. What he says next isn't a threat. It's a memory he was never supposed to have. Chain of Custody follows the hour a locked door stopped protecting the person who closed it, and the moment composure finally, quietly, breaks.
