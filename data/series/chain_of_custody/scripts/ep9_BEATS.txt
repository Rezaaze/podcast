4. Internally, he treats the breach not as a setback but as the visible cover he needed for what happens next.
5. The audience recognizes his stillness as the same patient control from the locked room, now simply repositioned.

5. The audience sees the two threads the previous episode kept separate now sharing a single spotlight.
