TITEL:
Mei Lin — Forty-Eight Hours to Break Him

BESCHREIBUNG:
The clock started the moment Daniel Wu disappeared, and it hasn't stopped since. When a tip pulls dockworker Jonas Voss into custody, Detective Mei Lin has forty-eight hours to turn silence into a confession — and every hour that passes tightens the noose around her. Anna Wu paces her brother's empty apartment, counting the same hours from the outside, refusing to let the case go quiet. Captain Sun's composure cracks for just a second when he hears the suspect's name, and a journalist starts circling a story that could blow the investigation open before it's ready. As the deadline closes in, Mei Lin makes a choice that has nothing to do with procedure. This episode of Chain of Custody is about the pressure of time running out, and what a person is willing to do — quietly, alone, behind a locked door — when the clock finally hits zero.
