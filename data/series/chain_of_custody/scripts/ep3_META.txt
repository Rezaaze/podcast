TITEL:
Mei Lin — What Voss Really Wants

BESCHREIBUNG:
A detail slips out that no rumor could have supplied, and Mei Lin realizes Voss knows more than he should. As he dangles fragments of a case she thought was buried, her leverage starts to feel like a leash. Meanwhile Kai Yang follows a trail of altered records into a file that predates everything he thought he understood, and finds himself watching Mei Lin with new, uneasy attention. Across town, journalist Zoe Han pulls at a loose thread in the precinct's finances, meets a retired dispatcher with a strange story about the docks, and photographs an empty stretch of water that shouldn't be empty. Captain Sun makes a call he doesn't want traced. Two searches, once separate, start converging on the same silence. When Mei Lin finally asks Voss what he wants, his answer isn't the one she braced for. Chain of Custody keeps tightening the wire between what people know and what they can afford to say out loud.
