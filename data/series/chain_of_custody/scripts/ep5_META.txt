TITEL:
Mei Lin — The Confession That Wasn't

BESCHREIBUNG:
Cornered until there's nowhere left to stand, Mei Lin says more than she means to — and Voss hears every word she didn't intend to give him. With the sign-in sheet gone, she's the only proof the lock-in ever happened, and that fragile fact becomes the pressure point Voss keeps pressing, circling closer to the Okafor case until exhaustion does what interrogation couldn't. Meanwhile Kai Yang unearths a backup Sun forgot to erase, Anna Wu follows her missing brother's last words to a bar on the docks, and what she overhears there ties Daniel to Voss's world in ways she never expected. As Zoe Han and Anna finally compare notes, the threads start pulling taut — for Mei Lin, for Sun, for everyone still pretending they're in control. This installment of Chain of Custody is about the moment leverage quietly changes hands, and how much can be lost in a single unguarded confession.
