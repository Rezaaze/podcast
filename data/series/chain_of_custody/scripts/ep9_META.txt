TITEL:
Anna Wu — The Room Was Never as Empty as It Looked

BESCHREIBUNG:
Anna Wu believes she is finally alone with the truth about her brother — and that belief is the mistake Voss has been waiting months to exploit. In this episode of Chain of Custody, the isolation Mei Lin engineered to protect the investigation becomes the exact gap in oversight her opponent needs to move. What begins as routine follow-up — a padlock, a gap in a schedule, one more question that should have been harmless — quietly rearranges who holds power in the room. Calls go unanswered at the worst possible moment. Two separate leads converge into something far more dangerous than either seemed alone. Every eye that should be watching is somewhere else. As the walls close in around a truth that was never meant to surface this way, the balance between hunter and hunted shifts so gradually that no one sees it happen until it already has. This is the episode where patience becomes a weapon, and the person searching for answers may not be the one asking the last question.
