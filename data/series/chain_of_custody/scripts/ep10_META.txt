TITEL:
Mei Lin — What the Room Cost

BESCHREIBUNG:
A detective's silence is about to cost her everything she built it to protect. In the season finale of Chain of Custody, the lock-in that began with one impossible choice finally breaks open—for Detective Mei Lin, for the captain who thought his ledger was buried, and for a family that has waited weeks without answers. As Internal Affairs closes in on Captain Sun and a fragment of evidence sends a raid team racing toward a container at the harbor, Voss watches every thread pull tight around the room he built. Anna Wu waits in a lobby that offers no comfort, bracing for whatever comes through the door. Across a single day, alliances collapse, confessions come too late to matter, and the cost of eight years of buried truth finally comes due. By dawn at the harbor, each of them will be left holding something different—a badge, a name, a reunion, or a debt no one above Sun will ever pay.
