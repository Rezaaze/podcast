--- SCENE 1 ---
1. Mei Lin enters the interrogation room with Voss, closes the door, and manually switches off the recording light before sitting down.
2. She states plainly that she wants one thing only: Daniel Wu's location.
3. Voss notices the recording light is off before she can hide the gesture, and files it away without comment.
4. Mei Lin tells herself this is a controlled, temporary measure she can end whenever she chooses.
5. Voss settles back, unhurried, signaling he intends to make her wait.

--- SCENE 2 ---
1. Kai Yang radios Mei Lin for her scheduled check-in and gets dead air.
2. He tries again a minute later, assuming a dropped signal.
3. He glances at the clock, noting the session started longer ago than usual for a routine check.
4. He decides not to escalate yet, chalking it up to Mei Lin running behind.

--- SCENE 3 ---
1. Voss answers Mei Lin's questions about Daniel Wu with vague, circling non-answers.
2. He reads her impatience and deliberately slows his pace further.
3. Mei Lin pushes harder, revealing how much the forty-eight-hour deadline is weighing on her.
4. Voss registers her urgency as leverage rather than pressure on himself.
5. Neither one says aloud that the room has no witnesses and no record — both silently understand it.

--- SCENE 4 ---
1. Kai Yang pulls up the interrogation room log on his terminal.
2. He sees no activity updates since the session began, well past the normal interval.
3. He checks his phone for a missed call or text from Mei Lin and finds none.
4. A small, specific worry replaces his earlier assumption that she's just running late.
5. He decides to walk over to the interrogation room himself instead of radioing again.

--- SCENE 5 ---
1. Kai Yang knocks on the interrogation room door and asks if everything's on track.
2. Mei Lin answers through the door with a quick explanation about needing a clean, uninterrupted run at Voss.
3. She does not open the door or invite him in, which is unlike her usual practice.
4. Kai Yang notices the explanation doesn't match the log's total lack of activity.
5. He agrees to leave her to it, but the exchange lingers with him as he walks away.

--- SCENE 6 ---
1. Kai Yang sits back at his desk, replaying the strange exchange at the door in his mind.
2. He decides not to mention his suspicion to anyone else in the bullpen yet.
3. He quietly pulls up Mei Lin's recent case activity on his own terminal, starting to look for anything unusual.
4. The audience sees him begin an informal, private inquiry that Mei Lin does not know is happening.

--- SCENE 7 ---
1. Voss shifts the conversation away from Daniel Wu without warning.
2. He says the name "Grace" quietly, almost offhand, and watches Mei Lin's reaction closely.
3. Mei Lin's expression flickers before she can control it, though she says nothing yet.
4. Voss recognizes the flicker as confirmation that the name means something to her.
5. The audience, primed by the case file, understands this is the first thread of leverage being pulled — Mei Lin does not yet know how much Voss actually has.

--- SCENE 8 ---
1. Mei Lin recovers and claims she doesn't know what Voss is talking about.
2. Her denial comes a beat too fast, betraying that the name did land.
3. Voss says nothing further on the subject, letting her own silence afterward do the work.
4. Mei Lin tries to redirect back to Daniel Wu, but her control over the room has visibly slipped.
5. The audience recognizes this as the first crack in the composure she believed she could maintain at will.

--- SCENE 9 ---
1. Captain Sun is informed, through a routine status update, that Mei Lin's session with Voss has run well past any authorized window.
2. He checks for an incident report or authorization for the extended session and finds none.
3. He considers stepping in, then decides to wait, telling himself it will likely resolve on its own.
4. Privately, he weighs how an inquiry into the room might expose favors and decisions of his own.
5. He chooses inaction, believing he still controls the timeline — not realizing Kai Yang is already digging.

--- SCENE 10 ---
1. Voss tells Mei Lin he doesn't just remember the name Grace, he has something written down about it.
2. He deliberately doesn't specify what the document is or where it came from.
3. Mei Lin presses him to clarify, and he declines, enjoying the ambiguity.
4. She tries to assess whether he's bluffing, but can't be sure.
5. The audience knows, from the case file, that his claim is real — records from a defunct dispatch operator — though neither character says so aloud.

--- SCENE 11 ---
1. Kai Yang's informal digging leads him to an old case file from eight years back.
2. He notices Mei Lin's name attached to it as lead detective.
3. He recognizes the case as the Grace Ito disappearance, a name he vaguely recalls from precinct history.
4. He opens the file further, beginning to read details he didn't know existed.
5. The audience understands he is now on a collision course with the exact secret Voss is currently using against Mei Lin, though the two threads haven't touched yet.

--- SCENE 12 ---
1. Voss finally says the full name aloud: Grace Ito.
2. Mei Lin's composure breaks for the first time in the session — her voice catches or she goes still.
3. Voss watches this reaction and understands he now has more power in the room than she does.
4. Mei Lin realizes she can no longer treat this as a conversation she fully controls.
5. The scene ends with the balance of the interrogation visibly reversed from where it began.
