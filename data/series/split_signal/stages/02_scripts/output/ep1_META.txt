TITEL:
Adrian — The Hours He Lost

BESCHREIBUNG:
Adrian wakes to discover hours he cannot account for, with only fragmentary images and the unsettling knowledge that something went catastrophically wrong. As his memory dissolves into darkness, three separate worlds collide: a father facing the finality of a custody hearing, partners bound by a secret that could destroy everything, and a man whose missing time might hide the worst of himself. Split Signal interweaves these colliding narratives into a night of psychological horror and moral reckoning—where the distance between innocence and complicity becomes impossible to measure. What begins as a deceptively simple question—where were you?—unravels into something far more dangerous: who are you when no one is watching, and what happens when you can't remember who you've become?

FRAGE:
How do you know when someone is hiding the truth from you—and what makes you decide whether to ask or let it remain hidden?
