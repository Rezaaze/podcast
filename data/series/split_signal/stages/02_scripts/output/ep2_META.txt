TITEL:
Captain Zhao — Control in the Spaces Between

BESCHREIBUNG:
Authority, like silence, can be weaponized. In this episode of Split Signal, Captain Zhao works to consolidate power over her task force by protecting a secret arrangement—one no one else knows exists, one that demands absolute loyalty. It requires false logs, careful documentation, and total control over information flow. Adrian's withdrawal deepens as his blackout becomes a locked door that Renata pounds against with hardening suspicion. Unauthorized visits pierce the boundaries Zhao has carefully maintained. Coded conversations mask intentions. Budget filings hide truth in plain sight. Behind the quiet consolidation of power, a conspiracy forms—subtle and patient, drawing in those who don't yet understand they're part of it, or why they're bound to protect it.
