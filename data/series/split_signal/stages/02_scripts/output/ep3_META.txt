BESCHREIBUNG:
When the body speaks, who listens? Adrian wakes at the abandoned Cannery, drenched in blood he cannot explain—a memory he cannot access. The same week, during a supervised visit, Mia suffers a blackout in front of her mother. By the time Renata's lawyer sees the incident report, something has shifted: the blackout is no longer just a medical mystery. It is evidence. Two blackouts. Two kinds of damage. Split Signal traces the fracture lines between memory and guilt, between what we can prove and what we're terrified to remember. As Adrian searches for answers only he can give, and as Renata's case tightens its grip around everyone who loves her, the cost of silence—and the price of confession—rises with each passing moment. This episode asks: when your own body becomes a stranger's evidence, whose story do you believe?
