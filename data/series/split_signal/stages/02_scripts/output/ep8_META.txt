BESCHREIBUNG:
A custody hearing reaches an unexpected turning point as buried secrets surface and alliances splinter. In Split Signal's most consequential episode yet, Adrian and Voss execute their final move against Toll's hidden infrastructure while a falsified funding trail emerges just far enough to shatter Zhao's career. Renata faces a ruling that changes everything about her path forward, while Piper confronts the full weight of accumulated deception—and what it has cost her, what it's cost them both, what it means now. The episode forces a reckoning where institutional corruption, personal betrayal, and the bonds between mother and daughter collide in ways neither could have anticipated. What emerges from the wreckage will reshape not just their lives, but everything that comes after.
