A redacted name. A hidden funding trail. When Piper's investigation into Zenith Horizon unearths a contract with critical information stripped away, she enters a maze of corporate silence and legal obstruction, chasing a phantom identity someone has taken extreme measures to protect. As she threads connections and digs deeper, each answer spawns new questions, each discovery draws her closer to a dangerous convergence. Split Signal's latest episode is a taut psychological thriller about the moment when investigation becomes risk, when the hunt for truth transforms into something far more dangerous. It's about institutional power and personal obsession colliding at a point of no return, and what happens when the line between seeking justice and inviting destruction gets dangerously thin.
