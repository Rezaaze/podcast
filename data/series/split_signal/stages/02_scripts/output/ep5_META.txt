BESCHREIBUNG:
Adrian's blackouts used to be just missing time. Now they're a nightmare made concrete. A recovered memory fragment and evidence of a victim he never met force an unbearable truth: he's not the only one steering his body. During his lost hours, someone else has been living his life, making his decisions, leaving traces only he can see.

Split Signal reaches the moment that breaks everything as Adrian investigates the impossible—the presence dwelling in his own mind. Not a voice. Not a memory fragment. A separate consciousness that moves through his body when the lights go out. As he traces the evidence through crime scenes and impossible gaps in time, Adrian begins to understand that the greatest threat he faces isn't hunting him from outside. It's been riding inside his skull the entire time. And now that he knows, the question is no longer whether he can survive this. It's whether he can live knowing what he contains.
