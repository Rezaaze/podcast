TITEL:
Lena — Hemmed in Secrets

BESCHREIBUNG:
Something isn't meant to be in that dress, and Ivy knows it the instant the needle catches. During Lena's final wedding fitting, a hidden object is discovered in the hem—and her sister's panicked reaction gives everything away before a question can be asked. As Lena pulls it free and Ivy's denials spiral into contradictions, a single text message on an unfamiliar screen changes everything. In Stitched, the hours before you walk down the aisle can unravel every certainty you've built. What lies sewn into the seams of a wedding dress isn't just a secret—it's a betrayal that someone went to extraordinary lengths to conceal, and now Lena must decide what happens next.
