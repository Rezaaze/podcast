TITEL:
George Pollard Jr. — The Captain the Whale Refused to Let Go

BESCHREIBUNG:
In 1820, the Pacific Ocean turned against a Nantucket whaleship, and what followed defied every rule of survival. When the whale ship Essex was shattered by a bull sperm whale twice its length, Captain George Pollard Jr. and his crew were cast into three open boats, thousands of miles from any shore, with dwindling water and no charts to salvation. What began as a fight against the sea became a descent into something far darker, as hunger reshaped morality and desperate men made choices no law of God or man could forgive. This episode of Dead Reckoning: The Captains the Ocean Unmade traces Pollard's unraveling from confident captain to haunted survivor, and the private ritual he carried for the rest of his life on land, unable to outrun what the ocean forced him to become.
