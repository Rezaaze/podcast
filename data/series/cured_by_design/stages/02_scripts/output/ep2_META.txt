Mei-Ling Chow — What the Polaroids Revealed

BESCHREIBUNG:
Two journalists find a Polaroid in a sealed archive. As they trace it through decades of institutional records, the path leads somewhere none of them expected—somewhere that changes everything. Cured by Design investigates the secrets hidden in archives and the agonizing choices that face those who find them. When Mira and Ethan uncover a connection between the hospital's history and a prominent family, they confront a question with no easy answer: How do you publish a truth that isn't yours to tell? This episode documents the moment when archival work becomes moral reckoning—when the facts in your hands could reshape someone's life. The team grapples with what they owe to the dead, what they owe to the living, and what any of us owes to history.
