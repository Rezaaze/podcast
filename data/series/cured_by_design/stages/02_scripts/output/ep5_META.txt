TITEL:
Cured by Design — When Paranoia Becomes Personal

BESCHREIBUNG:
Everything the team has fought to expose is under attack. Accounts locked. Dr. Pike receives a call—controlled, threatening, calculated. The external pressure tightens. But in Cured by Design, the real fracture isn't from outside the circle. It's within. Someone is meeting with the enemy. Mira knows something is wrong. She just can't prove it yet. As the team races to investigate while their systems burn, they're forced to choose: publish through the chaos and risk everything, or go silent. This episode traces the moment when paranoia stops being about the story and becomes about each other—when you can no longer trust the person in the room with you.

FRAGE:
When everything around you is under attack, how do you know who's still on your side?
