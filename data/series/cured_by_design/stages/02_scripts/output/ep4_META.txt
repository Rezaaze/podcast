BESCHREIBUNG:
In the same afternoon, a forced door and a political ultimatum reset every calculation. Dr. Pike returns home to find his apartment violated—one drawer emptied, contents missing. Across the city, Senator Marsh receives a visitor who explains exactly what her silence is worth. As Pike and his team scatter to a safe house, the walls begin to feel impossibly thin. Marsh faces her reckoning: evidence mounting, family ties deepening, and a choice that's stopped looking like a choice. In Cured by Design, safety is always an illusion. When pressure stops being theoretical and becomes very, very real, both the hunted and the compromised discover who they actually are.
