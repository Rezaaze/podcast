--- PART 1 ---

[NARRATOR]
A demolition crew cracks open a sealed time capsule inside a condemned nineteen-seventies asylum and finds a nurse's diaries and a stack of Polaroids of patients the record books call "cured" — and then vanished. A scrappy true-crime podcast starts pulling threads, and every thread leads to a name that now sits in a boardroom or a Senate seat. This is the story of what happens when the past is not just uncovered, but still very much in charge.
