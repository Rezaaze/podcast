TITEL:
Eleanor Voss — Secrets Sealed in the Walls

BESCHREIBUNG:
When demolition workers crack open a sealed wall at the asylum site, Mira and Ethan stumble onto something no one has seen in forty years: Eleanor Voss's diaries and fourteen Polaroids. A nurse's secret record of what she witnessed and what the institution wanted buried.

As they lay out the archive and begin matching faces to intake ledger numbers, a pattern emerges. But Ethan recognizes something in the photographs—something he doesn't immediately share with Mira. The investigation launches on two fronts: one visible, one hidden.

Cured by Design peels back the intimate, paranoid layers of a mystery where every discovery leads to new questions. Who were the fourteen faces? What was Eleanor trying to protect? And what is Ethan protecting by staying silent?

This episode marks the moment the evidence surfaces and the real work begins.
