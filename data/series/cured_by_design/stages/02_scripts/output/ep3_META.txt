BESCHREIBUNG:
An overnight deepfake of Mira spreads across the internet, initiating a coordinated attack designed to undermine Cured by Design's credibility and silence the show. But as the team mobilizes for damage control, Eleanor's painstakingly coded diary yields something far more intimate and dangerous: a name that resonates directly through Ethan's own bloodline. When the decrypted page vanishes from their shared archive and a forensics contact confirms the deepfake's professional caliber, the episode's true architecture emerges—a dual pressure mounting from both outside and within. The visible assault on the podcast's integrity masks an invisible crisis unfolding in parallel. What began as an external attack becomes a meditation on the distance between what you broadcast and what you must keep hidden.
