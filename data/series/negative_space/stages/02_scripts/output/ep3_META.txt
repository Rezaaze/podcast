TITEL:
Ethan & Mei — Trophies in the Dark

BESCHREIBUNG:
A string of coordinates finally resolves into a location, and what Ethan and Mei find waiting in the darkness of a storage unit changes everything about how they understand the case—and how personally invested in it they've become. But as the team begins to see the full scope of evidence, one careless favor from Marcus threatens to unravel the entire investigation from inside the forensics lab itself. Meanwhile, Yuki glimpses something in the new data she doesn't want to name, and Lina stands frozen at a crossroads between what she knows and what she can say. In Negative Space, each discovery brings new questions and new cracks in the trust that holds the team together. What was collected, and by whom, will force them all to reckon with how close the killer has been all along.
