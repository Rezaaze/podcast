TITEL:
Yuki — When Shadows Converge

BESCHREIBUNG:
The harbor fog rolls in thick and dark as Yuki coordinates the stakeout to catch the watcher before he strikes again. But as her team takes position around the riverside overlook, every relationship fractures under the weight of suspicion. In Negative Space, trust becomes dangerous. Ethan argues with Lina about joining the operation. Yuki suspects a leak inside her own team. And as the real watcher emerges from the shadows, the operation spirals beyond anyone's control. This episode explores what happens when loyalty collides with duty, when obsession demands sacrifice, and when the people closest to you become your greatest vulnerability. The fog doesn't lift. The machinery waits in the darkness. And no one can be certain anymore who they can believe.
