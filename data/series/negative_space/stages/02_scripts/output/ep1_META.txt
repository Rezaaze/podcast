TITEL:
Ethan — Twelve Frames, One Shadow

BESCHREIBUNG:
An unmarked hard drive arrives at intake. Inside: family photographs, ordinary and intimate—except for the shadow. The same shadow. In every frame. Negative Space follows Ethan as he discovers that some watchers prefer to remain unseen, and follows his team as they pursue a question that shouldn't exist: why is the same figure lurking at the edge of twelve different moments? As Lina guards a mysterious tip she won't explain, Marcus bristles on the outside looking in, and Han's world intersects with the investigation, a larger picture develops. Not of a crime scene—of a presence. The corkboard behind closed doors grows heavy with connections. And the question becomes not what happened, but who wanted it known, and why they trusted Ethan to see.

FRAGE:
What would you do if you found something in someone's memories that made you question the story they told about their own past?
