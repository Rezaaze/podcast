BESCHREIBUNG:
A second storage unit surfaces across the city, triggering a question that stops Ethan cold: how close are the dead to his own life? In Negative Space, trust fractures ripple through the forensics lab as Mei uncovers a pattern in the access logs, while Lina's source has begun asking about Ethan specifically. Han's darkroom lessons carry a weight beneath the surface, and the second trophy room's inventory maps uncomfortably close to Ethan's childhood. With each discovery, the case tightens—Marcus hesitates, relationships that held steady begin to slip, and the walls close in. Yuki returns to old case files. Lina walks home shaken. This episode traces the geometry of proximity and exposure: how close danger can get before you realize you're not watching the crime from the outside anymore—you're in its frame, with nowhere left to hide.
