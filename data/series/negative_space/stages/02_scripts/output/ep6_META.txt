TITEL:
Reflections — What the Glass Holds

BESCHREIBUNG:
In Negative Space, a single photograph becomes an accusation. Ethan's sanctuary has been breached—and the evidence lies hidden in a reflection frozen in glass, waiting to be decoded. As Mei works to extract what was captured there, pixel by pixel, the investigation spirals inward through the precinct itself, forcing Yuki to unearth a closed case she can no longer ignore and leaving Marcus scrambling to contain his role in the unraveling. The forensics lab becomes a pressure chamber where professional duty collides with personal betrayal. With each discovery, the team's carefully built trust begins to fracture under the mounting weight of exposure and suspicion, and the question at the heart of it all—what hides in that reflection—becomes impossible to ignore.

FRAGE:
Have you ever had your sense of safety shattered in an unexpected way—and how did it change your relationships?
