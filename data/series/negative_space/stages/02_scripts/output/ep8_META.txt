TITEL:
The Successor — When the Watcher Becomes the Watched

BESCHREIBUNG:
The violent confrontation should end everything. Instead, it begins the real crisis. When Ethan finally faces the person behind weeks of danger, what emerges is more complex than any investigation could be. In Negative Space, the aftermath demands its own reckoning. As the department processes an unexpected prisoner, as forensics traces evidence backwards through layers of intention, and as Ethan sits alone with truths that reframe everything he thought he understood, the case transforms into something far more personal. The discovery doesn't close wounds—it reveals how deep they run. The team that held together through external threats now faces the harder work: understanding complicity, accepting difficult truths, and deciding what remains when trust is tested in ways no one anticipated.
