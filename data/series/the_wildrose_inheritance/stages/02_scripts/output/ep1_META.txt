TITEL:
Lena — When Inheritance Becomes a Cage

BESCHREIBUNG:
The lawyer reads Werner Voss's will, and everything his family believed about their future begins to crack. His death has left the Wildrose Inheritance unresolved: the hotel split evenly between his two children, both signatures required to sell.

But Lena has already made a decision she hasn't spoken aloud. And when she names it—in front of her brother, in front of the lawyer—the ground shifts beneath every assumption Tobias has made about what comes next.

In this episode, the will becomes not a gift, but a cage. Tobias and his wife Julia sort through Werner's papers, finding questions no one was asking. On the boardwalk outside, Lena meets with an investor whose familiarity cuts deeper than any business deal should allow—a man who already knows her better than anyone else in the room realizes.

The inheritance has been distributed. The harder part is only beginning.
