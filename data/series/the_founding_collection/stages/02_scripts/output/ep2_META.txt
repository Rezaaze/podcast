Julian — When the Ledger Speaks

BESCHREIBUNG:
A ledger doesn't lie. But the people who read it might. When Nora shows Julian what she's found in the family accounts, it sets a private circle in motion—one that expands in dangerous directions. Julian meets Anna Lin off the record to understand the scope of what's hidden. Simon recognizes something in her face that he's carried for years, and chooses to say nothing. Marcus makes careful moves in the shadows, quietly buying up the family's financial vulnerabilities. What begins as one family's question about numbers becomes something far more complex: a meditation on loyalty, on who deserves trust, and on how long secrets can stay buried when everyone under one roof knows something they're not saying. In The Founding Collection, one wrong signature threatens to unravel the careful architecture of old money.
