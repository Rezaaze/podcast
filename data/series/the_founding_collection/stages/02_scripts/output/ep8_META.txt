TITEL:
Marguerite — The Ledgers Settle

BESCHREIBUNG:
Decades of silence are about to shatter. In this climactic episode of The Founding Collection, the Vantry family confronts the moment where everything hidden must be brought into the light. Marguerite makes a choice that cannot be undone: the family will speak its truth before it is spoken for them. What began as a carefully guarded secret about a 1951 forgery becomes public record, forcing each member of the family to face what they owe—to each other, to the past, and to those they've wronged. Marriages, inheritances, and the fragile bonds of family will be tested by the weight of finally being honest. The auction house becomes a battleground not just for money, but for who these people will choose to be. This is the episode where silence ends and reckoning begins.
