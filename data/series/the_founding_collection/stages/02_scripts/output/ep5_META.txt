TITEL:
Simon Lin — Fifty Years of Silence

BESCHREIBUNG:
Simon has kept fifty years of silence in his workshop, locked away in a single drawer. Tonight, he finally shows Anna the truth: correspondence and ledgers that prove the Founding Collection was built on a deliberate forgery, hiding a history far more complex and personal than anyone knew. He himself has carried this secret all these years. What Anna discovers in that moment cannot stay contained to her alone. The truth begins to spiral through the household—reaching others, each understanding something of what was hidden, each forced to reckon with it differently. The Founding Collection reaches its turning point in this episode, where buried identities reshape every relationship in the house, where certainty cracks under the weight of revelation. What unfolds is intimate and irreversible: a confrontation with family legacy, with the cost of protective lies, and with the impossibility of staying hidden. In one night, everything changes.

FRAGE:
What matters more—protecting people you love from a painful truth, or giving them the right to know it?
