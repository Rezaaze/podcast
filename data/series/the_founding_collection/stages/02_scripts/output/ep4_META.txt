TITEL:
Julian — When Secrets Cost Everything

BESCHREIBUNG:
This episode of The Founding Collection pulls at the threads connecting old money, old debts, and the lies we tell to protect the people we love. When a call arrives demanding payment on years-old obligations, Julian makes a choice he thinks he can control—a private loan arranged in shadow. But secrets this deep move through families in unexpected ways. Elsewhere, Anna's research into 1938 partnership papers is converging toward something the family has spent decades burying. The episode weaves between Julian's careful deceptions, Anna's dangerous discovery, Simon's private reckoning, and Marcus's patient orchestration of a debt that's been waiting decades to be called. It's a study in how family secrets work: layered, interconnected, and increasingly impossible to keep. By the end, nearly everyone knows something is wrong. Almost no one knows why.

FRAGE:
Have you ever kept a secret from someone you love thinking it would protect them, only to wonder if you were really protecting yourself?
