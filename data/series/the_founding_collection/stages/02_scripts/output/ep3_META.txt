TITEL:
Marguerite — What Stays Hidden

BESCHREIBUNG:
The Founding Collection tightens its grip when Marguerite moves to lock away the family's most dangerous secrets. Nora stumbles upon evidence that challenges everything she believed about her marriage. Marcus Tang returns with a second approach, his knowledge becoming a subtle but unmistakable threat. In a family built on old money and older debts, information becomes the most dangerous currency. As each character moves to protect what matters most—their secrets, their dignity, their standing—the carefully maintained boundaries of trust begin to fracture. This episode explores what happens when trying to contain the truth only pushes harder against the walls we've built, when knowledge itself becomes something to fear, and when those meant to safeguard us must decide what they're willing to risk to keep our family's secrets.
