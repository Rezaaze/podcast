TITEL:
Jonah — The Message in the Static

BESCHREIBUNG:
A watermark hidden in corrupted security footage becomes the first tangible lead—but the signal that emerges carries a troubling symmetry to images no one was supposed to see. As the forensics team works to understand what the footage captured, a trauma therapist pursues a quieter investigative path, drawing fragments of memory from the boy who drew the same symbol weeks before. The two threads converge, accelerating the investigation, but so do the doubts: who is guiding which questions get asked, and whose boundaries are being protected? Meanwhile, those in control of information begin to move more deliberately. In Amity Hollow, every answer opens new lines of pressure, and trust is becoming the rarest resource.

FRAGE:
How do you decide who to trust when the person guiding the investigation might benefit from certain answers and not others?
