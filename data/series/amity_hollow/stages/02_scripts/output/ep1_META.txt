A forensics team arrives at Amity Hollow and finds the village frozen mid-moment: string lights still glowing, dinner tables abandoned, every resident vanished without explanation. At the center stands Milo, a boy found alone and silent, his walls covered in symbols he refuses to explain.

As the team begins their investigation—seizing encrypted devices, searching the darkened homes—internal fractures surface. Jonah needs this case to prove himself. Yuna navigates restricted zones with suspicious ease, raising questions about her loyalties. Mei Lin attempts to reach a traumatized child who has withdrawn into absolute silence. Wei Zhou discovers something in the seized data that cuts too deep to ignore.

Over one long night, the mystery deepens: corrupted video files, unexplained firmware updates, whispers at the treeline. But the real question isn't what happened to the residents. It's what Milo knows, and what's making everyone in the team—even the people standing beside you—act like they have something to hide.
