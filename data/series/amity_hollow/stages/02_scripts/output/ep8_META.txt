TITEL:
Old Chen — What Emerges From Below

BESCHREIBUNG:
The team's escape route is cut off by guards, and survival hinges on one desperate push: commandeering a satellite uplink van and broadcasting the raw footage of Amity Hollow before the security net closes. Wei Zhou wires the corrupted feed while Jonah guards the entrance. Old Chen's voice crackles over the emergency channel, guiding them from memory alone through tunnels and time collapsing toward zero. Yuna faces an impossible choice between her exit plan and the team's only chance. When the broadcast finally goes live, reaching every open channel in range, the world learns what Halcyon Vantage built—and what Old Chen lost building it. In that moment of exposure, with federal agents moving and executives scrambling, the cost of truth becomes suddenly, terribly clear.
