BESCHREIBUNG:
The blast doors won't open. Trapped underground with secrets that someone paid to keep buried, a team confronts not just walls of steel and concrete, but the fracturing trust between them. As they search for a way out, the bunker itself reveals what was meant to stay hidden—and the implications ripple outward, touching everyone connected to Amity Hollow. Old debts emerge, hidden families are revealed, and a figure from the past steps forward with knowledge that changes everything. Each character will face a choice between the lies that protect them and the truths that destroy them. This episode strips away the illusions that held the ensemble together, forcing them to confront who they really are to each other. Survival means seeing everyone clearly—and clarity, it turns out, comes at a cost that may be too high.
