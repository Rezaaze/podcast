TITEL:
The Signal — Voices Below the Static

BESCHREIBUNG:
When Wei Zhou isolates a repeating signal buried beneath Amity Hollow's smart-home audio logs, the investigation lurches forward — but the frequency carries a weight he wasn't prepared to bear. As technical evidence points toward answers about the vanished, Old Chen surfaces with a past he won't fully explain, and the team finds themselves circling the same questions with decreasing trust in one another. In this episode of Amity Hollow, discovery doesn't bring clarity. Instead, it deepens the isolation, forcing each character to reckon with what they know, what they're willing to admit, and who — if anyone — deserves the truth. Some signals carry the weight of histories never meant to be found. A waveform doesn't lie. But it doesn't speak the whole story either.
