BESCHREIBUNG:
Following an order to stand down, the team moves through the night toward a restricted checkpoint. What Wei Zhou discovers in the guard station—evidence binding the land directly to a corporation called Halcyon Vantage—forces a reckoning none of them anticipated. Smart-home hubs. Corporate supply chains. A badge system registered to Meridian Sentry Services. As they trace the connections, a darker question emerges: who knew? Yuna's carefully measured reasoning begins to crack. Milo spirals at a single word. And when thermal imaging reveals ventilation venting from undeveloped land on every map, Old Chen studies the drone footage and goes very still. His recognition is immediate. Whatever waits beneath the ridge, he's already mapped the distance. In Amity Hollow, the most dangerous discoveries aren't the secrets you uncover—they're the ones you realize were hidden inside your trust all along.
