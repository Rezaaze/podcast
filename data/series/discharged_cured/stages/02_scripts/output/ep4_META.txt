TITEL:
Maya and Elias — Everything But a Name

BESCHREIBUNG:
The silence surrounding Hollow Pines is finally breaking — but only in whispers. In this episode of Discharged, Cured, Maya sits down with Mei Tam, who has held pieces of the truth for forty years. What emerges is careful, fragmented, always stopping just short of the names that Maya and Elias have been hunting. Meanwhile, Elias pursues sealed hospital records from the 1970s in the county archive — records that might contain everything. The archive is slow to respond. Delays accumulate. As Maya absorbs the weight of Mei's careful words and Elias races to photograph evidence before it becomes inaccessible, the investigation accelerates into dangerous territory. They uncover fragments of the cover-up, secure documentation they thought was lost, but the central name — the identity at the heart of everything — remains just beyond reach. Danger is now personal. Time is not on their side.

FRAGE:
