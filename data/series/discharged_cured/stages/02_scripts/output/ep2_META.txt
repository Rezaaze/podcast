TITEL:
Maya Park — A Trail of Erasure

BESCHREIBUNG:
Every name on the Polaroid should lead somewhere—a birth record, a death certificate, a trace. None do. In *Discharged, Cured*, Maya and Elias methodically cross-reference the entire list against public records and discover something far worse than answers: systematic erasure. Ten patients documented in photographs, ten names absent from every archive. As their investigation narrows to a single surviving lead—a retired nurse from Hollow Pines—the atmosphere shifts. Tao Wu begins his own quiet inquiry. Diana positions the podcast as a threat worth containing. And Maya's personal crisis deepens when her mother finally responds to weeks of unanswered calls—only to offer fragments of a family secret about the grandmother she's never been told about. The only path forward leads through a door that's been shut against them.

FRAGE:
How do you decide when it's worth pushing for answers, and when it's time to walk away?
