TITEL:
Daniel — What the Lake House Knows

BESCHREIBUNG:
A family secret decades in the making shatters in a single night when Daniel arrives at the lake house with questions no one wants to answer. Miles away, Mei Tam prepares to finally share a memory she's kept hidden for fifty years—one that reframes everything Maya thought she understood about her own family's past. In Discharged, Cured, two parallel investigations converge in the same moment of revelation. An old ledger, a hidden diary, and one unexpected name are the threads connecting a decades-old cover-up to everyone it touched. When truth can no longer be contained, the cost of silence falls due.

FRAGE:
How far would you go to protect a family member's secret, and at what point does loyalty become complicit?
