TITEL:
Maya — What the Ground Gave Up

BESCHREIBUNG:
Hollow Pines Hospital's demolition crew discovers a sealed time capsule from 1970: diaries, photographs, records of lives the institution erased. When Elias brings the find to Maya, she recognizes something in those old Polaroids that stops her cold—a name that shouldn't be there, attached to a face she knows intimately. In Discharged, Cured, the investigation into the 'Buried Patients' begins as professional curiosity but transforms into something far more personal. As Maya and Elias uncover the pattern—missing death certificates, unmarked graves, a carefully managed silence—someone in Senator Diana Chen's political orbit is already taking notice. This episode traces the collision between a decades-old cover-up and the present-day people whose careers, families, and reputations depend on keeping it buried. When the ground gives up its secrets, some people will move mountains to put them back.
