TITEL:
Mei Tam — What's Left to Lose

BESCHREIBUNG:
When a story threatens institutional power, the machinery of suppression locks into place. Diana Chen orchestrates a public offensive while Tao Wu weaponizes the law, flooding the investigation with cease-and-desist letters and legal threats. Everyone involved feels the pressure: Daniel is pulled from his beat, Maya and Elias receive their own warning, and a frightened source appears with crucial information, then vanishes. But it's Mei Tam who faces the tightest corner—confined to Riverside, her visitors restricted, caught between her fear of complicity and the knowledge she carries. She must decide what to protect and what to release, knowing that every choice narrows the path forward. In Discharged, Cured, institutional silence has never felt so suffocating, and one person's decision could shift the entire investigation.

FRAGE:
At what cost would you keep a dangerous secret to protect yourself?
