Diana Chen — When Warmth Becomes a Weapon

Diana Chen has agreed to an on-record interview about Hollow Pines—a bid to control the narrative before it controls her. In this episode of *Discharged, Cured*, that single interview triggers a cascade of pressure.

Diana arrives impeccably composed, each answer precisely calibrated. Two rival journalists—Maya from the podcast and Daniel Chen from *The Ledger*—meet for the first time, and their professional opposition kindles into unexpected warmth.

But offstage, tension spirals. Mei Tam grows rattled by the broadcast. Tao Wu quietly works old networks. Daniel is pushed to dig into a family he never wanted to expose. By episode's end, fragile truces form and hidden files are examined—but every truth revealed is another threat waiting to surface.
