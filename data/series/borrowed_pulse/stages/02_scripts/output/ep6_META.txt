TITEL:
Maya Lin — The Old Signal Answers

BESCHREIBUNG:
A code phrase from another lifetime moves through the underworld, searching for a woman who may not be who everyone thinks she is. When Maya Lin hears the words meant only for the Quiet Man, something in her answers—half-conscious, unstoppable, betraying her in a single breath. In Borrowed Pulse, this moment of recognition triggers a cascade: at the clinic, records are being destroyed; at the precinct, a warrant is finally signed; and in the spaces between, everyone is closing in. The surgeon faces impossible choices. The detective plays his last card. The syndicate confirms what they need to know. By the waterfront's edge, Maya will confront the consequences of being recognized as something she never chose to be—and discover who else is watching in the dark.

FRAGE:
Have you ever kept a secret from someone so close to you that it felt like you were living two lives?
