TITEL:
Maya — A Heart That Remembers Another Life

BESCHREIBUNG:
Maya wakes into a body that isn't hers—a chest housing a heart that knows things she doesn't, that moves before she decides to move. In the recovery room, nurses evade her questions about the donor. At home, Daniel welcomes her back grateful and unknowing, unable to see that something fundamental has shifted. But on the harbor's edge, a body surfaces bearing the exact signature of a killer Detective Calloway thought was finished. And in the quiet corners of the Night Market, a name from a closed past begins to move again. Borrowed Pulse traces the first days of Maya's strangeness, Daniel's devotion shadowed by secrets, and the collision of an unsolved death with a woman learning to live inside a borrowed body. When the heart beating in your chest isn't entirely yours, every heartbeat is a betrayal waiting to happen.

FRAGE:
If saving your life meant keeping a secret from the person closest to you that would change how they saw you forever, could you really live with it?
