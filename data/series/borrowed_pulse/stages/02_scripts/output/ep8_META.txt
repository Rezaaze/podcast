TITEL:
Chen — When Mercy and Murder Wear the Same Face

BESCHREIBUNG:
When the revelation Chen has been hunting finally surfaces, every single alliance fractures and every truth becomes a weapon. Daniel corners Maya for a confession she cannot fully give—she hides secrets about who she truly is and where she came from. Calloway orchestrates a raid into the clinic's hidden records, pursuing connections the syndicate desperately wants kept buried. And Sora carries knowledge dangerous enough to save the people she loves or doom them entirely. In Borrowed Pulse, competing truths collide and clash across the vendetta's machinery, the marriage's betrayals, and the surgeon's final morning before everything changes forever. Multiple investigations converge and tighten from every direction. Every thread pulls inward toward the center. Everyone's running out of time.

FRAGE:
How much of the truth do you owe to someone you love—and how much can you hide and still call it love?
