TITEL:
Maya — Hands That Aren't Her Own

BESCHREIBUNG:
In Borrowed Pulse, the body keeps its own secrets—and so do the people we love. Maya wakes to find her hands moving with a precision she never learned, her reflexes sharp enough to unsettle her. As she navigates her marriage and daily work, fragments of another consciousness flicker at the edges of her awareness—not quite a voice, not quite a memory. Meanwhile, Sora discovers something in dockside footage that makes her throat tighten, and the city's clinic begins erasing records of an identity no one is supposed to know about. This episode explores the terror of inhabiting a body that's becoming a stranger, and the lies we tell those closest to us to keep them safe—or to keep ourselves intact.

FRAGE:
If you discovered something strange happening to your body, what would make you keep it secret from the person you love most?
