When buried secrets break into the open, the syndicate's fragile world fractures entirely. Sora corners Maya on the container line with the truth about the transplant—a revelation that dismantles everything they believed about her and themselves. As lies collapse under the weight of accumulated evidence, each character confronts an impossible choice: maintain the comfortable fiction they've carefully constructed or reckon fully with everything they've overlooked for far too long. This is the episode where the body speaks its truth, the ledger demands its answers, and the entire foundation of trust stops holding completely. What happens when recognition becomes absolutely unavoidable?
