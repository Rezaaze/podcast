--- PART 1 ---

--- PART 2 ---

[NARRATOR]

[TOMMY_LAU | style: gritted patience]

[SFX: a metal file drawer sliding open on a dry track]

[TOMMY_LAU | style: muttering]

[AUGUSTINE_KWOK | style: low, resolved]

[SFX: a metal box latch clicking open then shut]

[TOMMY_LAU | style: stunned, barely audible]

[AUGUSTINE_KWOK | style: steady, unmoved]

[TOMMY_LAU | style: shaken]

[NARRATOR]

--- PART 3 ---
