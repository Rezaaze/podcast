TITEL:
Nora — Alone at the Threshold

BESCHREIBUNG:
Nora reaches nine months, but she's never carried a moment like this entirely by herself. The Amaranth Clock's countdown reaches its most private milestone: an ultrasound appointment she keeps entirely to herself, telling no one. In these hours—from slipping out unnoticed to reading the numbers on the screen to the long walk back before dark—Nora is forced to reckon with the ledger of her own choices and the weight of what she's carrying. This is a story about the intimacy of solitude, the clarity that arrives when there's no one else there to interpret what you see, and the particular kind of courage it takes to hold still and listen to your own life.
