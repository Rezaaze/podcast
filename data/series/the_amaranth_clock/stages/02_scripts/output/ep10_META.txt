TITEL:
Augustine — Two Truths, One Breath

BESCHREIBUNG:
Two truths converge in Augustine's final act — one hidden in numbered columns, one measured in the months he has left. As the Amaranth Clock ticks toward the auction's gavel fall, Iris moves to expose the machinery Julian built in shadow, while Augustine finally opens the fireproof box, reading Walter and Henry's careful fraud aloud to those who loved them and those it shattered. But in the same conversation where he speaks this truth, he tells Lila something harder still: he is dying. Meanwhile, the season's other question — about Nora, about fatherhood, about time itself — remains suspended, deliberately unresolved. This is the episode where two endings meet at precisely the same breath, where the ledger is read, and where one beginning refuses to come.
