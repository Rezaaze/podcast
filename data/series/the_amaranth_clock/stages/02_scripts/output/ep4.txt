--- PART 2 ---

[SFX: a shelf hinge creaking as a folder is pulled free]

[SFX: a stack of folders settling against a metal shelf edge]

[SFX: a heavy cardboard box being dragged across a concrete floor]

[NARRATOR]

[AUGUSTINE_KWOK | style: light, masking something underneath]

[AUGUSTINE_KWOK | style: barely audible, alone]

--- PART 3 ---

[NARRATOR]
Day eight since Tommy's release, twenty-two days now until the Amaranth's auction closes out. [IRIS_TANAKA | style: breezy]

[SFX: a stack of mail set down on the desk]

[DESMOND_HALE | style: mild, thinking aloud]

[IRIS_TANAKA | style: politely curious, not alarmed]

[DESMOND_HALE | style: careful, still puzzling it]

[IRIS_TANAKA | style: unconcerned, already moving on]

[DESMOND_HALE | style: shrugging it off, warm]

[IRIS_TANAKA | style: fond, already turning to go]

[SFX: the elevator bell dings once, down the hall]

[NARRATOR]

Desmond watches her go, still turning the discrepancy over, no closer to guessing whose hand is behind it — or that the man due at three is on his way from a diner booth across the street, where someone else is about to get far too close a look at him.
