--- PART 1 ---

[NARRATOR]
In the lobby of the Amaranth, the wall clock has read 4:47 for ten years, and nobody has bothered to fix it — because in this building, nothing is ever really over. Six residents. Four countdowns already running when we arrive: thirty days until the building goes to auction, six months into a pregnancy nobody can quite explain, three days since a man came home from twelve years in prison for a fire he swears he didn't set, and a diagnosis one resident is keeping from everyone he loves. Each of them knows their own piece. None of them know the whole shape of what's coming. This season, we watch the clock.
