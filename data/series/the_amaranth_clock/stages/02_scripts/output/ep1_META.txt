TITEL:
Nora — When Silence Becomes Consent

BESCHREIBUNG:
When Tommy comes home, Nora makes a choice that will reshape the next nine months. She has three days to tell the neighborhood the truth about the baby's father. She doesn't. Meanwhile, in the archive's quiet stacks, Augustine performs a routine task that amounts to recommitting to silence—a secret she thought was safely buried. The Amaranth Clock is a show about season-long continuity and the exact moments when people decide what stays hidden. This episode traces what happens across three days and one night when small choices lock secrets permanently into place. The most dangerous lies are the ones that don't require defending, because everyone has already decided they're true.

FRAGE:
Have you ever let a misunderstanding about you become accepted truth rather than spend the energy explaining the complicated reality?
