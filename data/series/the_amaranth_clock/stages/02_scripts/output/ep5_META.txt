TITEL:
Augustine — Witnessed

BESCHREIBUNG:
When private shame becomes impossible to hide, the stories we've built to hold everything together require urgent revision. The Amaranth Clock finds two people at the same turning point: Augustine faced with a moment of visible vulnerability, watched by someone who sees; Desmond investigating the structural lies beneath an institution everyone trusted. What begins as a stumble in a greenhouse becomes a crisis that demands a new narrative — a secret that was meant to stay buried finds its first real cover story. Across eight interlocking chapters, watch as relationships bend under the weight of truth, as foundations reveal themselves to be fabricated, and as the network of careful explanations begins to fray.
