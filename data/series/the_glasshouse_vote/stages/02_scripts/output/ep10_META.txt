TITEL:
Mei-Lin — What the Roof Remembers

BESCHREIBUNG:
On the morning of a vote that will reshape their lives forever, the Glasshouse cooperative finally faces what it has spent years avoiding: the truth. Aiko's calculated deception. Declan's mounting debts. Soo-Jin's borrowed identity. Each secret that surfaces transforms the question before them—how do you choose what kind of family to be when that family has sheltered so many lies for so long? In The Glasshouse Vote, you'll witness people grappling with impossible choices, watching as the community reckons with honesty and consequence. Some truths lead to understanding. Others remain buried, known only to those who comprehend their true cost. The vote awaits. Everything after depends on it.

FRAGE:
When honesty threatens the bonds that hold a community together, which do you protect—the truth or the family?
