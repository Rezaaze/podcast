The dawn arrives with the coring test—but the night before, everything shifts. Aiko finally tells the truth she's carried for years, a truth about the structure at the heart of their community and the long-ago injury that shaped them all. Across the compound, Mei-Lin stands alone at the pier, haunted by what she knows and the choice before her. In this episode of The Glasshouse Vote, the line between loyalty and complicity grows thin. When people we trust most have been keeping secrets, when the foundation we stand on is built on careful omissions and old wounds, what does it mean to be honest with each other? This night asks impossible questions about truth, accountability, and the cost of finally being known by the people closest to us.
