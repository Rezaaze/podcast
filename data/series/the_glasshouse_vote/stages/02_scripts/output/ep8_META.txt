TITEL:
Mei-Lin — Two Lies to Carry

BESCHREIBUNG:
The Glasshouse Vote reaches a critical inflection point as carefully constructed facades begin to crumble under mounting pressure. Soo-Jin's fragile situation shifts from private board knowledge to open speculation among residents, thrusting her into the kind of visibility she never wanted. Simultaneously, a surprise financial audit forces Declan to confront a lie that no longer holds—his cover story unraveling with every questioned entry. At the heart of it all, Mei-Lin finds herself at an impossible crossroads: the mandatory coring test is closing in, and she'll do whatever it takes to postpone it one more time, even if that means risking exposure of the secret she's guarded most fiercely. This episode captures the cascading moment when a tightly knit community's carefully maintained silence begins to fracture, and four residents discover they cannot carry their lies alone any longer.

FRAGE:
How far would you go to protect someone you care about, even if it puts you at risk?
