TITEL:
Mei-Lin — The Practiced Walk-Through

BESCHREIBUNG:
Iggy Chen arrives with a clipboard and an easy smile, asking Mei-Lin for just a look around, nothing official. But in the super's workshop below, Aiko's hands are shaking for the first time in years. In the co-op office, Declan reaches for the tidiest folder he can find. And in the greenhouse, Mei-Lin remembers the routes through citrus rows like she's preparing a dance she's never rehearsed. The Glasshouse Vote has always been a place where people know too much about each other and choose carefully what to say. Today, that becomes everything. An afternoon of small talk and careful silences unfolds as a walk-through that tests not just the building's integrity, but the thin threads of trust that hold everyone inside it together.
