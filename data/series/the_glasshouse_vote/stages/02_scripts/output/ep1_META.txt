TITEL:
Mei-Lin Castellan — A Fortune on the Roof

BESCHREIBUNG:
A buyout offer arrives at the Castellan Glasshouse Cooperative, and the board gathers for a vote they thought they wanted. Instead, four members of this century-old ensemble discover that wealth, when it finally comes, doesn't simplify anything at all. In this episode of The Glasshouse Vote, an emergency meeting reveals a number so staggering that it stops everyone's breath. But beneath the shock, each character retreats into their own secret arithmetic—calculating what they stand to lose, not gain. Watch as old certainties begin to crack under the weight of sudden fortune, as quiet agendas reshape how the vote unfolds, and as the question on the table becomes not whether to sell, but what it means to belong to something worth more than money.
