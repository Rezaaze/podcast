TITEL:
Soo-Jin — When Identity Becomes Paperwork

BESCHREIBUNG:
A routine legal request becomes a threat. A minor audit discrepancy becomes a cover story. In *The Glasshouse Vote*, the ordinary machinery of bureaucracy—forms, signatures, verification letters—turns an ordinary day into a collision between who we are and what our records say. Soo-Jin receives a letter asking for proof of identity. Declan discovers a flagged invoice and scrambles to explain it. Iggy's questions keep pressing. And the residents of a co-op—people who share walls, share space, share life—start to sense that something fundamental is cracking. This episode explores the weight of paper trails: the small inconsistencies that grow into larger deceptions, and what happens when the official record tells a different story than the person in front of you.
