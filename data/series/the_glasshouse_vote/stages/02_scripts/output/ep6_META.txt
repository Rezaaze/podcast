Mei-Lin — Before the Truth Surfaces

BESCHREIBUNG:
A structural inspection is coming to the southeast pier, and Mei-Lin has one week to choose what she's willing to lose. In The Glasshouse Vote, carefully buried secrets are about to be tested—literally and figuratively. As Halloran's announcement reverberates through the building, each resident faces a reckoning: Mei-Lin grapples with an impossible choice at the pier, Aiko weighs whether confessing first might protect her from what inspectors will uncover, and Declan's fabricated invoices begin to crumble under scrutiny. Soo-Jin, too, feels the pressure as her secrets inch toward exposure. In lobbies and apartments, kitchens and workshops, nine people confront the same terrifying question: Do you confess and take control, or stay silent and hope? This episode is about the moment when running out of time forces you to choose.
