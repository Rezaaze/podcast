TITEL:
Liwei — What Waits Below

BESCHREIBUNG:
In The Glasshouse Vote, a simple request becomes dangerous. Liwei volunteers to assemble shareholder records and maintenance archives for a legal matter—a task so mundane it seems harmless. But inside those boxes and ledgers lie the decisions that shape a building, and the secrets its residents have worked to keep buried. As Liwei begins his work, others watch with growing unease: his mother steering him away from the oldest files, a neighbor protecting invoices that tell their own story, a longtime resident weighing whether a story she knows—about the night the roof came down, about repairs that happened in silence—should stay silent. What the records officially say and what they conceal are not the same thing. Sometimes the most ordinary tasks unearth the most guarded truths.
