TITEL:
Liwei Castellan — What the Silence Keeps

BESCHREIBUNG:
As the vote edges closer, pressure mounts on every side. Liwei Castellan pushes the co-op and his own mother to move faster, convinced that hesitation is simply caution from an older generation. But in The Glasshouse Vote, timing conceals as much as it reveals. Declan moves swiftly to organize and 'streamline' the co-op's financial records before outside scrutiny arrives. Across the building, residents work quietly through the evening—Mei-Lin among the rows at dusk, Soo-Jin studying old identification documents by lamplight, Aiko reinforcing the structure after hours. Each action is deliberate. Each silence carries weight. When Liwei surfaces a decades-old folder marked 'ARCHIVE—PRE-1970,' he begins to understand that the slowness he's been fighting may not be resistance at all, but protection.
