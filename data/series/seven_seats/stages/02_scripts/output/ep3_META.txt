TITEL:
Jonah — Fury Without Reason

BESCHREIBUNG:
On day three of the evacuation, when Hurricane Odette upgrades to Category Three, the island makes its first impossible choice. In Seven Seats, survival has a price nobody wants to pay. A fabricated mechanical fault cuts the ferry's capacity by one seat. Fury builds in someone who doesn't understand why. A gentle question receives a lie so precisely constructed it might feel like mercy. And somewhere in the clinic archive, one name gets crossed off the evacuation list—the first of many compromises to come. This is what it looks like when necessity wears the mask of routine, when good people make bad choices, and when silence becomes a form of violence. The cover stories hold. For now.
