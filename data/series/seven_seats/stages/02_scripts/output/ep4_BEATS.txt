--- SCENE 1 ---

5. Sophie watches him closely, her earlier doubt about his back-injury story still unresolved beneath the day's rush.
6. The audience registers the countdown accelerating in lockstep with the storm itself, tightening every thread at once.

--- SCENE 2 ---
1. Sophie volunteers to pull requisition paperwork from Aldridge's office so the next supply run can be finalized quickly.

3. Sophie searches through stacked folders on his desk, focused only on supply forms and manifests.
4. She works quickly, aware she's on a short errand and not looking for anything beyond the requisition list.
5. The audience recognizes this ordinary task is about to intersect with the secret Aldridge believes is still contained.

--- SCENE 3 ---

4. Sophie realizes the mainland trip Aldridge described as a supply run was actually this diagnosis six weeks ago.
5. She says nothing and tells no one, deciding in the moment to hold what she's found rather than act on it yet.
6. The audience learns Sophie now knows the truth Aldridge has hidden from everyone on the island, though he has no idea she's found it.

--- SCENE 4 ---
1. Sophie returns to rounds beside Aldridge within minutes of reading the letter, the requisition folder still in her hands.
2. She keeps her voice and manner level, matching his brisk storm-prep pace as if nothing has changed.
3. Aldridge, unaware anything has shifted, treats her as he did that morning, focused only on the day's tasks.
4. Sophie catches herself studying his movements differently now, seeing exhaustion and pain where she once accepted a back injury.
5. She says nothing to him, holding the secret in silence through the rest of their rounds together.
6. The audience feels the imbalance sharpen: Sophie now carries the truth Aldridge believes is still his alone.

5. The audience feels the collision arriving: Kimi's question is the direct one Mei has feared since the season began.
