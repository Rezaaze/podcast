TITEL:
Kimi Sato — The Weight of Six

BESCHREIBUNG:
Hurricane Odette is strengthening offshore, and the conversation in the clinic turns to survival: the ferry has only six guaranteed seats. As Dr. Aldridge announces the evacuation plan, quiet preparations begin throughout the community. Kimi Sato is asked to draft the priority list—a task that requires choosing between neighbors and friends. Danny Su makes his own calculations down at the dock. Mei Chen reaches back into the clinic's archives. Sophie Kang watches her mentor for signs she can't quite name. In this episode of Seven Seats, as the storm closes in and resources grow scarce, every character makes a hidden choice about what they'll sacrifice, what they'll hide, and how they'll live with themselves when there isn't room for everyone.

FRAGE:
When you can't help everyone, how do you decide who gets what little you have?
