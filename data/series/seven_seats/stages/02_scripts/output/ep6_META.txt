TITEL:
Kimi — When Duty Becomes Mercy

BESCHREIBUNG:
Hurricane Odette is making landfall, and the final ferry is nearly full when the clinic staff discovers the manifest doesn't match the people waiting to board. In *Seven Seats*, Kimi must make the impossible decision of who leaves Halyard Cay alive—while confronting truths about her mother's death and the cost of mercy under catastrophic pressure. When the person standing in front of you, asking for help, carries both your refuge and something you're only just learning to grieve, fairness and compassion demand different things. As the storm closes in and the ferry engine runs, there is one seat remaining, and Kimi must choose. Not who deserves it. Just who leaves, and who stays.

FRAGE:
If you discovered something devastating about someone you needed to trust in a moment of crisis, how would that change your choices?
