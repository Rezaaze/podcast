TITEL:
Kimi — When Twenty-Two Years Finally Crack

BESCHREIBUNG:
As Category 4 Odette barrels toward the coast, the clinic's carefully maintained facade begins to crumble under the weight of its own buried secrets. Sophie uncovers a truth about Aldridge that she cannot share while standing beside him in the hours before the storm strikes. Across town, Kimi moves methodically through evacuation records and the clinic's decades-old archive, cross-checking names and dates—until she finds a phrase that shouldn't exist, evidence of something deliberately hidden for more than two decades. When old wounds surface under pressure and trust fractures at the exact moment the community needs it most, no one at the clinic can afford to break. Seven Seats explores what happens when the ground beneath your certainty gives way.

FRAGE:
When someone chooses silence over honesty—even to protect you—is that an act of care or a betrayal of trust?
