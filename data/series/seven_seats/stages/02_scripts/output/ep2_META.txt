TITEL:
Aldridge — what the margins can bear

BESCHREIBUNG:
As Hurricane Odette's outer bands draw closer, the island doesn't just brace for weather—it braces against itself. A supply run at the ferry shed nearly connects two people who cannot meet. An insurance form forces a woman to relive the exact words she altered in a file twenty-two years ago. A doctor's private pain surfaces through his hands, unmistakable to anyone watching closely. On Seven Seats, secrets don't announce themselves—they leak through footprints in sand, through stories invented to deflect truth, through a grip on an exam table that tells its own story. This episode follows how close people come to exposure, how carefully they step around each other, and what the body reveals when the voice stays silent. By nightfall, every secret still stands. But the island's margins have closed.

FRAGE:
If you caught someone struggling with a secret, would you push them toward honesty, or respect their right to keep it?
