BESCHREIBUNG:
The outer bands of Odette arrive ahead of schedule, and every carefully laid plan on Halyard Cay runs out of time. In Seven Seats this week, long-held secrets break open under pressure—names are finally spoken, a diagnosis is said aloud for the first time, and a hidden presence is discovered when you're not looking. A doctor confronts an impossible choice about his own future. A young woman finally hears the answer to a twenty-two-year-old question. Two brothers stand in a ferry shed and watch everything they've built on silence start to crack. And in the clinic, a woman with a clipboard discovers that the numbers she's been counting don't add up the way anyone hoped. As the island braces for impact, the real crisis isn't whether the boat will hold everyone. It's whether anyone can survive what has to come next.
