TITEL:
J. Marion Sims — The Silver Sutures and the Screams History Silenced

BESCHREIBUNG:
In a converted slave cabin in 1840s Alabama, a surgeon built the foundations of modern gynecology on the bodies of women who had no power to refuse him. This episode of Anatomy of the Damned: Physicians Who Butchered to Heal traces the ascent of J. Marion Sims, a physician consumed by a singular obsession: curing the agony of vesicovaginal fistula, a childbirth injury that left women in ceaseless suffering and social exile. To perfect his technique, Sims turned an outbuilding into an experimental operating theater, performing dozens of procedures without anesthesia on enslaved women — Anarcha, Betsey, Lucy, and others whose names medical history nearly erased. Follow the forging of silver sutures, the rising toll of failed surgeries, and the uneasy machinery of consent under slavery, as one man's relentless pursuit of a cure hardens into a legacy the modern world is still struggling to reckon with.
